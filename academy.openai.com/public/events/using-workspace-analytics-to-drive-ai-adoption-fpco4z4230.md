# Deployment & Adoption

Join the ChatGPT Product + Customer Education team for a hands-on session on how teams use Workspace Analytics in ChatGPT Enterprise to run stronger rollouts—finding where adoption is gaining traction, where it’s stalling, and what to do next.

You’ll work through practical examples based on common rollout scenarios: using adoption benchmarks and trends to set a baseline, identifying pockets of high-impact usage, and turning those signals into targeted enablement actions.

**Who it’s for:** Workspace owners, admins, and AI champions responsible for driving ChatGPT Enterprise adoption.
