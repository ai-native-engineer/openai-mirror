# On the Air with AI: Behind the Scenes at TBPN

[Replay](https://academy.openai.com/home/clubs/news-organizations-b9osl/videos/on-the-air-with-ai-behind-the-scenes-at-tbpn-2026-05-22)

# Awareness

# Personal

AI in the newsroom is no longer about dabbling with prompts. The best teams are using it where editorial work moves fastest: spotting angles, shaping coverage, and turning daily chaos into repeatable systems.

In this 45-minute session, we’ll go behind the scenes with TBPN, the daily live tech and business show that produces three hours of programming every weekday. We’ll show how AI helps make that pace possible, and what any editorial team can learn from it.

Attendees will leave with practical ways to get started using AI in their own day-to-day work, regardless of technical background, And we’ll save time at the end for your questions!
