### This session is presented by our community partner, Autoblocks.

*This resource was produced by Autoblocks, a trusted partner in AI education. While OpenAI Academy shares it here, it does not necessarily represent OpenAI’s own views.*

As LLM applications move from prototypes to production, the stakes get higher — and trust becomes critical. Building safe, reliable, and scalable AI products isn’t just about model quality — it’s about having the right evaluation frameworks in place from the start.

In this OpenAI Academy session, Autoblocks will break down why evaluations (evals) are essential for any team serious about shipping trustworthy AI. We’ll share practical workflows, lessons learned from real-world deployments in healthcare and finance, and show how to create evaluation processes that keep pace with rapid development.

Whether you’re just starting to productionize LLMs or looking to scale safely, this session will give you tactical strategies you can apply immediately.
