## How to showcase use cases of ChatGPT for your team

September 23, 2025 · Last updated on May 29, 2026

![Leading impactful ChatGPT Trainings](https://cdn.gradual.com/images/https://d2xo500swnpgl1.cloudfront.net/uploads/oaiacademy/Admins-Cover-Images-22--4032b27a-d3af-40ea-90b8-b234d834699b-1758213628708.jpeg?fit=scale-down&width=1200)

Internal champions are critical to the success of ChatGPT Enterprise. As trusted, influential voices, champions help bridge the gap between potential and practical use, showing others what’s possible through curiosity, creativity, and a willingness to lead by example.

You can be a leader on your team by helping team members understand where generative AI could have the most impact in their role. You’re uniquely positioned to do this because you are an expert in both ChatGPT and your own company and team!

Check out the video below to see how to put together an impactful ChatGPT demo for your team.
