## Capture expectations, comfort levels, and where your team hopes ChatGPT can help.

![Baseline survey template](https://cdn.gradual.com/images/https://d2xo500swnpgl1.cloudfront.net/uploads/oaiacademy/Admins-Cover-Images-19--c97ebad7-4edb-4b61-8819-95fe8bd9decc-1754408041962.jpeg?fit=scale-down&width=1200)

# **Baseline Survey: Getting Ready for ChatGPT Enterprise**

*Tip: Use ChatGPT to modify this survey, or suggest additional questions based on your goals.*We’re preparing to roll out ChatGPT Enterprise. Before launch, we’d love a quick snapshot of how you currently work, where AI could help, and any concerns. This takes about 4 minutes. Your input helps us tailor training and measure impact over time. Thank you!

## Demographics

**1.** **Work email** ***(Optional if anonymous; Required if tying to usage)***

What is your work email?

**2. Department / function** ***(Required)***

Which department or function best describes your role?
**Options:**

* Finance

* Sales / GTM

* Marketing

* Operations / Support

* Product

* Engineering

* IT

* HR / People

* Legal / Compliance

* Other (please specify)

**3. Role level** ***(Required)***

Which best describes your role level?

**Options:**

* Individual contributor

* People manager

* Executive / senior leader

* Technical (engineering / IT / data)

* Other

## Predicting ChatGPT usage

**4. Expected ChatGPT uses** ***(Required; multi-select)***

How do you expect to use ChatGPT in your work?
**Options:**

* Research

* Writing & editing (docs, slides, emails)

* Data / analysis & insights

* Coding & technical support

* Brainstorming & idea development

* Automating routine tasks (summaries, formatting, etc.)

* Visuals / media support

* Learning & upskilling

* Other (please specify)

**5. Expected weekly time saved** ***(Required)***

If you start using ChatGPT regularly, about how much time would you expect it could save you per week?
**Options:**

* 0 hours

* Less than 2 hours

* 2–5 hours

* 5–10 hours

* More than 10 hours

* Not sure

## Additional considerations

**6.** **Attitudes toward AI tools** ***(Required; 5-point scale)***Please rate your agreement with each statement about using AI tools (like ChatGPT) at work.
**Scale:**

* Strongly disagree

* Disagree

* Neither agree nor disagree

* Agree

* Strongly agree

**Statements:**

* ChatGPT could help me be more productive

* ChatGPT could improve the quality and creativity of my work

* ChatGPT could increase my job satisfaction

* I feel confident I can learn to use AI tools effectively

* I feel comfortable trying AI tools on real work tasks

### **7. Projects you’d take on with more time or skill** ***(Optional)***

What kinds of tasks or projects do you wish you could take on, but don’t today due to time or skill limits?

### **8. Concerns about using AI** ***(Optional)***

Do you have any concerns about incorporating AI into your workflow? (e.g., accuracy, data privacy, change to role, other)

### **9. Top expectation from rollout** ***(Optional)***

What’s the #1 thing you want from our ChatGPT rollout?
Thanks! Your responses will help us roll out ChatGPT in a way that supports real work. We’ll follow up with training resources and tips soon.
