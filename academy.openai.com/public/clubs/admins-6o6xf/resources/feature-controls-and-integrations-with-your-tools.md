# **Feature controls and integrations with your tools**

Learn how to configure workspace features, control integrations and custom GPT sharing, and monitor usage through dashboards and compliance tools.

## Workspace settings

See the overview below of the different toggles that Workspace Owners will see on the [Settings page](https://chatgpt.com/admin/settings) for ChatGPT Enterprise workspaces. *Note that the ChatGPT Business workspace is more limited a to which workspace-wide settings you can control as an Owner.*

* **Canvas:** Canvas is an interface within ChatGPT for writing and coding projects that require editing and revisions. ChatGPT Enterprise workspace admins can control whether canvas **code execution** is available for users in the workspace along with the default network access behaviors. See: [Executing Python Code within Canvas](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/9930697-what-is-the-canvas-feature-in-chatgpt-and-how-do-i-use-it#h_29f6cf134e).

* **Web search:** Web search allows ChatGPT and GPTs to answer questions on recent topics. When enabled, it may send search queries and general location information to Bing, but this data is not linked or associated with user accounts. See: [ChatGPT Search for Enterprise and Edu](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/10093903-chatgpt-search-for-enterprise-and-edu#h_5888443d42).

* **Voice:** Manage whether members can share their screen or video while having a voice conversation (advanced voice on iOS and Android mobile apps only). See: [Sharing in voice mode](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/8400625-voice-mode-faq#h_ed91284f59).

* **Record:** Manage whether members can use ChatGPT Record, a feature that turns meetings, brainstorms, or voice notes into organized knowledge it can act on. See: [ChatGPT Record](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/11487532-chatgpt-record)﻿

* **Codex:** ChatGPT Enterprise owners can manage whether members can use Codex, OpenAI’s cloud-based software engineering agent. See: [Codex in ChatGPT](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/11369540-codex-in-chatgpt)﻿

* **Work with apps:** ChatGPT for macOS can now work with your apps, starting with coding tools like IDEs, terminals, and Apple Notes. See: [Work with Apps on MacOS](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/10119604-work-with-apps-on-macos).

## **GPT settings**

Both ChatGPT Enterprise and ChatGPT Business plans allow creation of **custom GPTs** (bespoke instances of ChatGPT with custom instructions or knowledge). See [GPTs FAQ](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/8554407-gpts-faq) - note that governance controls vary between ChatGPT Enterprise and ChatGPT Business workspaces. GPT settings can be found under the [GPTs tab](https://chatgpt.com/admin/gpts) in your Admin panel.

* In ChatGPT Enterprise settings, admins can set the **maximum sharing scope** for GPTs created by workspace users – e.g. restrict sharing to *“workspace only” or “private invite only,”* disabling public sharing. [Users on the ChatGPT Business plan](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/8798620-gpts-chatgpt-team-version) are able to share GPTs without admin approval.

* OpenAI allows third-party connections that connect ChatGPT to external services via **Custom GPT actions**. In an Enterprise setting, you can [disable or limit usage](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/9442513-gpt-actions-domain-settings-chatgpt-enterprise) for your workspace for security or other reasons.

* ChatGPT Enterprise and ChatGPT Business workspaces allow limiting **use of third-party (public or external) GPTs**.

**Connectors:** Both ChatGPT Enterprise and ChatGPT Business workspaces have the ability to connect to your third-party applications or your internal systems to enrich responses from ChatGPT, and admins and owners are able to manage connectors in the [Connectors tab](https://chatgpt.com/admin/ca) in the Admin panel. See: [Connectors in ChatGPT](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/11487775-connectors-in-chatgpt)﻿

## **Other considerations**

Set up a plan for ongoing monitoring.

* **Analytics dashboards:** ChatGPT Enterprise provides an **analytics dashboard** for usage insights (e.g. total messages sent, active users) that Admins and Owners can access in workspace settings. Identify who on your team will review these metrics. [**See more guidance on how to interpret and action on these analytics**](https://academy.openai.com/home/clubs/administrators-6o6xf/resources/chatgpt-enterprise-user-analytics-guide)**.**

* **Audit Logs via** [**Compliance API**](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/9261474-compliance-api-for-enterprise-customers)**:** ChatGPT Enterprise workspaces can access the **Compliance API** that gives an audit log of conversations and custom GPT activity. For example, you might have compliance or security engineers run scripts to fetch conversations metadata or flags.
