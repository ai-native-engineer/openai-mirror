## SUMMARY

AI Essentials for Journalists is a practical introduction to the fundamentals of using ChatGPT. This training covers core capabilities like prompting, document analysis, research, data analysis, and creating a custom GPT. With demos purpose built for newsrooms, this session focuses on practical application through experimentation, responsible use, and building confidence with AI tools.
