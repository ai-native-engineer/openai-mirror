## SUMMARY

In this video, you’ll discover the foundations of Prompt Engineering. The first step to unlocking ChatGPT’s full potential for content creation. Whether you’re writing scripts, generating ideas, or building a personal brand, the way you prompt makes all the difference.

Here’s what you’ll learn:

* The 4 elements of a powerful prompt: Role, Task, Context, and Constraints.
* How to give ChatGPT expert-level direction to get precise, creative outputs.
* Real-world examples of effective prompts for viral content and storytelling.
* The secret to personalising ChatGPT with Custom Instructions so it always matches your style and goals.
* How to make ChatGPT behave like your personal content assistant that remembers your tone, preferences, and needs.

This video is perfect for content creators, marketers, students, or professionals who want to use AI smarter, faster, and more creatively. By the end, you’ll be able to write prompts that deliver exactly what you need every single time.
