## SUMMARY

In this final chapter, you’ll learn how to use ChatGPT to analyse your social media analytics and optimise your content strategy. Instead of getting lost in spreadsheets, you’ll discover how to let AI do the heavy lifting so you can focus on creating content that actually works.
Here’s what you’ll learn:

* How to export and upload your analytics data (YouTube, Instagram, etc.) into ChatGPT.
* Ways ChatGPT can visualise trends, spot growth spikes, and calculate key metrics like engagement rate, CTR, and retention.
* Real examples of using ChatGPT to identify top-performing content, best posting times, and high-retention formats.
* How AI can suggest new topics, strategies, and trends tailored to your audience.
* How to connect ChatGPT with Google Sheets, Excel, Docs, and even Gmail for a fully integrated workflow.
  By the end of this chapter, you’ll know how to treat ChatGPT not just as a content creation assistant, but as your personal data analyst helping you grow smarter, faster, and more strategically.
