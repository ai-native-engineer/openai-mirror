## SUMMARY

In this chapter, you’ll discover how to use ChatGPT’s video calling and screen sharing features to get real-time, face-to-face AI assistance. From troubleshooting editing software to improving your lighting setup on a shoot, this tool is a game-changer for creators who need instant, visual guidance.

Here’s what you’ll learn:

* How to access and set up ChatGPT’s video calling mode on your device.
* Why video calling is more powerful than text when you need immediate, practical help.
* How to use the screen sharing feature for live, visual troubleshooting.
* Real-world use cases for creators:
  ✅ Fixing issues in editing tools like Premiere Pro
  ✅ Competitor video analysis in real time
  ✅ On-shoot lighting and setup feedback
  ✅ Instant design critiques for thumbnails, posters, and more
* How video calls with ChatGPT save time, reduce back-and-forth, and give you personalized, actionable advice.

Whether you’re editing, shooting, designing, or analyzing content, this chapter will show you how to use ChatGPT as your always-available creative partner.
