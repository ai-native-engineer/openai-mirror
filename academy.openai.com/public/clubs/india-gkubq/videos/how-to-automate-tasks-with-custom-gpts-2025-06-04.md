## SUMMARY

In this video, you’ll learn how to use the free GPT Store to automate repetitive tasks and how to build your own personalised GPT Bot without writing any code.

Here’s what you’ll learn:

* What GPTs are and how they’re different from normal chats
* How to browse and use ready-made bots from the GPT Store
* How to create your own GPT with custom instructions and files
* A step-by-step walkthrough of using both Create and Configure modes to create a GPT Bot.

Whether you're a student, teacher, content creator, or business owner, this lesson will help you build your own AI assistants and save time on everyday work.
