## SUMMARY

In this video, you'll learn what an AI agent is and how to build one. You'll discover when to use agents, understand the core components, explore single-agent and multi-agent orchestration patterns, and learn about safety guardrails.

Target Audience and Technical Background:

* Developers and engineers new to AI agents who want foundational knowledge
* Technical professionals looking to understand agent architecture and design principles
* Tech Leads and Software architects evaluating when and how to implement agent-based solutions
* Startup founders, CTOs and Product managers who need to understand agent’s fundamentals
* Students and researchers seeking comprehensive introduction to agents

Prerequisites: Basic understanding of LLMs.
