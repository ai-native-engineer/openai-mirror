## SUMMARY

In this video, we’ll explore how to use ChatGPT to write professional-quality scripts for YouTube videos, Instagram Reels, and video ads. With the right structure and techniques, ChatGPT can become your personal scriptwriter, editor, and creative partner all rolled into one.

Here’s what you’ll learn:

* Why choosing the right model makes a huge difference in scriptwriting.
* A step-by-step method to structure your script prompts for maximum clarity and creativity.
* How to customize scripts by specifying video type, number of people, duration, platform, and tone.
* The secret power of adding references (links, scripts, or videos) to get perfectly aligned results.
* How to build your own Custom GPT assistant that always remembers your tone, structure, and style.
* Why a dedicated GPT for scripts saves time, ensures consistency, and boosts content quality across formats.

This video is perfect for content creators, digital marketers, and aspiring storytellers who want to level up their videos with compelling, platform-ready scripts. By the end, you’ll have a clear framework and even your own personal scriptwriter inside ChatGPT.
