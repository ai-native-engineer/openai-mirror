## A custom GPT that guides you through creating prompts, step-by-step.

December 5, 2025 · Last updated on May 29, 2026

![ChatGPT Use Cases for Work GPT](https://cdn.gradual.com/images/https://d2xo500swnpgl1.cloudfront.net/uploads/oaiacademy/Academy-content-covers-28--2452715e-d4f7-4bab-9280-0be930864e73-1764948941372.jpeg?fit=scale-down&width=1200)

*This content for small business owners was a part of the Small Business Jam and created in collaboration with our partners at DoorDash.*

This GPT guides you through creating prompts step-by-step. It’s great if you’re new to prompting or not sure where to begin.

**What it helps with:**

* Figuring out useful AI tasks for your business

* Asking you clarifying questions

* Turning your answers into clear, ready-to-use prompts

* Creating simple workflows you can reuse

**When to use it:**

* As you begin learning the foundations of ChatGPT ([ChatGPT 101 for Small Businesses](https://academy.openai.com/public/clubs/small-business-ipf4m/videos/chatgpt-101-introduction-to-chatgpt-for-small-businesses)/[ChatGPT 102 for Small Businesses](https://academy.openai.com/public/clubs/small-business-ipf4m/videos/chatgpt-102-for-small-businesses) are good places to start!)

* If you get stuck designing or building out processes and solutions for your small businesses using ChatGPT
