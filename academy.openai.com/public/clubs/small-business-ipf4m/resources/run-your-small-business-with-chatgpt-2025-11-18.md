## Hands-on examples to help you write better prompts, faster

![Small Business Prompt Pack](https://cdn.gradual.com/images/https://d2xo500swnpgl1.cloudfront.net/uploads/oaiacademy/Academy-content-covers-2--123430da-2bfa-4281-b301-ee8e1f1cc805-1763653190291.jpeg?fit=scale-down&width=1200)

*This content for small business owners was a part of the Small Business Jam and created in collaboration with our partners at DoorDash.*

As a business owner, you juggle planning, budgeting, customer relationships, marketing, and operations—all while keeping your company growing. ChatGPT can help draft proposals, marketing copy, and customer communications; summarize contracts and reports; organize project plans; explore new opportunities; and even generate templates for finances, HR, and compliance. With AI handling the busywork, you and your team can stay focused on strategy, growth, and customer success.

This guide is meant to help:

* Introduce how ChatGPT can assist an employee’s day-to-day work

* Provide guidance on responsible AI use

* Offer high-level guidance on how to prompt large language models

* Identify specific prompts that will assist users who are interested in using ChatGPT to accelerate their work
