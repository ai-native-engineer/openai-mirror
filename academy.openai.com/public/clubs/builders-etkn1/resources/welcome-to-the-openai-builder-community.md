# **Welcome to the OpenAI Builder Community**

Welcome to the Builder Community: a curated space on OpenAI Academy featuring resources hand-selected by OpenAI specialists to help you quickly build practical, real-world AI applications. Here you'll find essential artifacts, grounded tutorials, and structured learning resources tailored for builders at every stage of their journey.

## **AI techniques videos**

Stay up-to-date on the latest techniques shaping AI application development. These videos ground you in the hottest topics, providing clear, practical insights into key AI methods:

* **Building with the Responses API:** Quickly build dynamic interactions and responses using OpenAI’s Responses API

* **Building agents:** Learn the essentials of creating intelligent agents that leverage tools, models, and memory effectively.

* **Launching applications with evals**: get up to speed with evaluations to ensure your applications deliver high-quality, reliable outcomes.

## **Solution accelerators**

Accelerate your development process with expert-curated frameworks and patterns designed to simplify complex implementations. Quickly adopt proven methods for:

* **Recommendations Accelerator**: Architect and deploy powerful recommendation systems.

* **Coding Agent Accelerator**: Streamline agent-driven coding solutions.

* **Customer Service Accelerator**: Implement efficient, intelligent customer support systems.

* **Voice Solutions Accelerator:** Design and deploy real-time, natural-sounding voice interfaces powered by GPT models.

## AI App Development Learning Track

Explore the full track here: <https://developers.openai.com/tracks/ai-application-development>﻿

This structured e-learning program guides you from **concept to production**, ensuring you can build robust, scalable AI applications. It’s organized into four parts:

* **Introduction -** orientation, environment setup, and how the track works.

* **Phase 1: Foundations** - core concepts of prompting, embeddings, and model behavior.

* **Phase 2: Application Development** - building prototypes with RAG, agents, and APIs.

* **Phase 3: Testing & Evaluation** - designing evals, applying guardrails, and measuring performance.

* **Phase 4: Scalability & Maintenance** - deploying at scale, optimizing latency and cost, monitoring, and iterating.

## **Recommended navigation path**

To get the most from these resources, consider this recommended approach:

1. Start with the [**AI Techniques videos**](https://academy.openai.com/home/clubs/builders-etkn1/tags/ai-techniques-6877d65781524bc22944d151) if you're new or looking to refresh your knowledge.

2. Progress through the comprehensive [**AI Application Development track**](https://academy.openai.com/home/clubs/builders-etkn1/externals/ai-app-development-concept-to-production-2025-08-18) to build a strong foundation and structured approach.

3. Dive deeper using our **Solution Accelerators** and [additional learning tracks](https://academy.openai.com/home/clubs/builders-etkn1/tags/technical-learning-tracks-68a3cb3bb6735037ee532c79) on developers.openai.com to enhance your knowledge across specialized AI use cases and techniques.

Welcome aboard, let’s start building.
