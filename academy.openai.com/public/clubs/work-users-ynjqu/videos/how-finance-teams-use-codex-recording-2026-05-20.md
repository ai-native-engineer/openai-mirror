## SUMMARY

Codex can help finance teams move beyond simple prompts by turning scattered context from spreadsheets, forecasts, meetings, and business updates into useful outputs like variance narratives, dashboards, executive briefs, and repeatable workflows. In this beginner-friendly finance webinar, you’ll learn what to hand off to Codex, how to review its work with confidence, and where to find finance-specific resources and use cases to keep building momentum.
