## SUMMARY

Codex can help BizOps teams turn scattered operating context into the kinds of artifacts leaders actually use: dashboards, weekly updates, decision briefs, follow-up notes, and repeatable workflows. In this beginner-friendly webinar, we’ll show how a BizOps team can use Codex to bring together data, files, and team context, check the work, and shape it into a clear operating story. You’ll see examples like building a marketplace health review, drafting a leadership-ready update, and turning a recurring process into something easier to run each week.
