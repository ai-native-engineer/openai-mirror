## SUMMARY

Codex can help sales teams move beyond simple prompts by turning scattered context from CRM records, account notes, meetings, emails, and pipeline updates into useful outputs like ranked prospecting plans, outbound-ready research, meeting briefs, follow-up drafts, and next-step assets. In this beginner-friendly sales webinar, you’ll learn what to hand off to Codex, how to use the new Sales plugin, how to review its work with confidence, and where to find sales-specific resources and use cases to keep building momentum.
