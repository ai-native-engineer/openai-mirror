## SUMMARY

Codex can help data science teams move beyond simple prompts by turning scattered context from dashboards, notebooks, spreadsheets, metric definitions, and stakeholder notes into useful outputs like analysis readouts, dashboard QA checklists, executive slides, and repeatable workflows. In this beginner-friendly data science webinar, you’ll learn what to hand off to Codex, how to review its work with confidence, and how to turn data work into decision-ready outputs your team can refine and reuse.
