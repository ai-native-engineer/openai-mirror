## **Solution Kit for Financial Services**

This Solution Kit brings together the essential resources financial institutions use to evaluate, adopt, and scale AI in real-world, regulated environments. Whether you’re exploring initial use cases or supporting teams already deploying AI, these guides, tools, and examples are designed to help you move faster with confidence.

All resources are built specifically for the needs of banks, asset managers, insurers, and other financial services organizations. Learn more about [OpenAI for Financial Services](https://openai.com/solutions/industries/financial-services/).

### **Prompts for ChatGPT Enterprise**

A curated set of ready-to-use prompts vetted for day-to-day financial services work, including:

* Data analysis and financial modeling

* Research, search, and synthesis

* Policy, tax, and regulatory interpretation

* Contract, covenant, and document analysis

* Data extraction and support for Excel, BI, and ERP workflows

These prompts are built to accelerate time-to-value while maintaining clarity, consistency, and auditability.

→ [Get ChatGPT Enterprise prompt packs](https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/b96a6047-53b2-43bd-85b9-44885cf4007a%20/chatgpt-enterprise-prompt-pack-for-financial-services.pdf)

### **Pre-built GPTs**

GPTs are purpose-built AI assistants that follow a defined set of instructions, rules, and guardrails—so analysis and decisions are consistent by design. We’ve built three example GPTs to help you get started. GPTs are easy to create, and can be built by anyone.

* **KYC / AML Risk Screener GPT:** Support customer due diligence and risk screening with structured analysis and consistent decision support. → [Get GPT](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-69389cdd6630819180059b57e9ff0062-kyc-aml-risk-screener)

* **Policy Interpreter GPT:** Analyze and interpret internal policies and regulatory text with traceable, explainable outputs. → [Get GPT](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-69389da64f78819180276f05e4241f36-policy-interpreter-gpt)

* **Investment Research Assistant GPT:** Performs structured, source-backed investment and market research using multi-turn web analysis. → [Get GPT](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-69389d6d21f481918007b589a33f25bf-investment-research-assistant)
