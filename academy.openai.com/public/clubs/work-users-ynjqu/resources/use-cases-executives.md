## ChatGPT for executives

Executives are focused on setting strategic direction, making high-stakes decisions, and ensuring alignment across teams. Their time is limited and attention is pulled in many directions, making speed, clarity, and leverage critical. ChatGPT helps executives cut through noise, allowing leaders to move faster, stay more informed, and make sharper decisions.

## Investor & market intelligence

*ChatGPT researches capital flows, valuation, sentiment, and transaction benchmarks to inform executive and investor decisions. Use* ***deep research*** *for deeper real-time insights.*

## Strategic planning & decision support

*ChatGPT helps develop multi-horizon strategies and structured comparisons to guide prioritization and resource allocation. Use a* ***reasoning model*** *for more strategic insights.*

| Use case | Prompt | URL |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Create a pricing strategy brief | We’re revisiting our pricing strategy for [product/service]. Based on [insert context: goals, customer segments, competitive positioning], suggest 2–3 pricing models and pros/cons of each. | ﻿[Try it in ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=We%E2%80%99re%20revisiting%20our%20pricing%20strategy%20for%20%5Bproduct%2Fservice%5D.%20Based%20on%20%5Binsert%20context%3A%20goals%2C%20customer%20segments%2C%20competitive%20positioning%5D%2C%20suggest%202%E2%80%933%20pricing%20models%20and%20pros%2Fcons%20of%20each.)﻿ |
| Prioritize growth levers | Given our goals [insert business goals], identify 3 high-potential growth levers and estimate effort vs. impact. Include a table with short descriptions and trade-offs. | ﻿[Try it in ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=Given%20our%20goals%20%5Binsert%20business%20goals%5D%2C%20identify%203%20high-potential%20growth%20levers%20and%20estimate%20effort%20vs.%20impact.%20Include%20a%20table%20with%20short%20descriptions%20and%20trade-offs.)﻿ |
| Analyze market entry risks | We are considering entering [new market/region]. Based on current economic, legal, and competitive factors, summarize key risks and mitigation strategies in bullet format. | ﻿[Try it in ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=We%20are%20considering%20entering%20%5Bnew%20market%2Fregion%5D.%20Based%20on%20current%20economic%2C%20legal%2C%20and%20competitive%20factors%2C%20summarize%20key%20risks%20and%20mitigation%20strategies%20in%20bullet%20format.)﻿ |
| Reframe strategic trade-offs | We’re choosing between [Option A] and [Option B] for our next big investment. Compare trade-offs across cost, time, team capacity, and customer impact. Show the metrics driving this and recommend 2 actions based on the findings. | ﻿[Try it in ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=This%20is%20a%20dataset%20of%20performance%20across%20%5Bregions%2Fproducts%2Fcustomers%5D.%20Identify%20which%20segments%20are%20over-%20and%20under-performing%20relative%20to%20the%20average.%20Show%20the%20metrics%20driving%20this%20and%20recommend%202%20actions%20based%20on%20the%20findings.)﻿ |
