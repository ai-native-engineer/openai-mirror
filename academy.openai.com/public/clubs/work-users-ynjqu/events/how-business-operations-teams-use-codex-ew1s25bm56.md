# How business operations teams use Codex

# Codex for Work

# Codex

Business operations teams are often the connective tissue between strategy, execution, reporting, and decision-making.

Codex can help teams pull scattered context into useful deliverables that clarify what’s happening, what needs attention, and what should happen next.

Join us for **How business operations teams use Codex**, a practical session on where Codex can fit into business operations workflows. We’ll keep the session high level while the demo details are still taking shape, focusing on how teams can gather context, shape outputs, and review quality before sharing.

In this webinar, we’ll cover:

1. Where Codex can fit into business operations workflows
2. How to turn scattered context into useful deliverables
3. How to review and refine Codex output

This session is designed to help business operations teams understand what’s possible and leave with starter prompts they can adapt as their use cases become clearer.
