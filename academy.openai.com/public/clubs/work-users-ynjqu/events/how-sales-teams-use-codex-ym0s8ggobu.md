# How sales teams use Codex

# Codex for Work

# Codex

Sales work depends on context that’s often spread across accounts, meetings, notes, emails, and pipeline updates.

Codex can help sales teams turn that scattered context into useful deliverables your team can review, refine, and act on.

Join us for **How sales teams use Codex**, a practical session on where Codex can fit into sales workflows. We’ll keep the session focused on high-level patterns: bringing account context together, shaping useful outputs, and reviewing quality before anything gets shared.

In this webinar, we’ll cover:

1. Where Codex can fit into sales workflows
2. How to move from scattered context to useful deliverables
3. How to review and refine Codex output

This session is designed to help sales teams see what’s possible and leave with starter prompts they can adapt once their use cases are clearer.
