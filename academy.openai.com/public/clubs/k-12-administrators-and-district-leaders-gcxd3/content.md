K-12 Administrators & District Leaders

Join community

As a District Leader, you guide the vision for how AI enhances teaching, learning, and operations across your schools. In this role, you’ll explore strategies for building district-wide readiness, modeling responsible AI use, and empowering educators to innovate. By connecting policy, professional development, and practical classroom applications, you’ll help establish systems that make AI a trusted partner in your district’s long-term success.
