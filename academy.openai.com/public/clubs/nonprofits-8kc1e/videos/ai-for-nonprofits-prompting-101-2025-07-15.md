## SUMMARY

This video teaches foundational LLM prompting techniques demonstrating clear structures, formatting, and best practices to improve outputs. This is geared towards general nonprofit staff new to AI, seeking foundational skills to improve their ChatGPT interactions and results. You can access the custom GPT mentioned in this video here: <https://chatgpt.com/g/g-kXxPg653x-structured-prompt-generator>

AI Impact Hub is an online community built for non‑profit professionals, where practical AI skills are learned through courses, ongoing learning and supportive peers. Members embed AI and automation into their workflows, reclaiming up to ten hours a week from repetitive tasks and reinvesting that time in the purposeful work that matters most.
