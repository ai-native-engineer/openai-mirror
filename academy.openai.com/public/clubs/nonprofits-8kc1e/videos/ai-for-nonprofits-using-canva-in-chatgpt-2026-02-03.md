## SUMMARY

This video explores ChatGPT's new Apps feature, demonstrating how to connect external tools like Canva directly within the ChatGPT interface. Learn how to create social media graphics and presentation slide decks without switching between applications, then open and fully customize your designs in Canva. Registered nonprofits can access Canva Pro for free, making this a powerful combination for resource-limited teams.
