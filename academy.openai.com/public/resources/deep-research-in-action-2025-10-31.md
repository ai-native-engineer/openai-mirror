## Exploring the next decade of U.S. health care

![Lauren Oliphant](https://cdn.gradual.com/images/https://d2xo500swnpgl1.cloudfront.net/uploads/oaiacademy/E03025Z7DT4-U08N36M5L7Q-6510f753fd0d-512-0bd0eccc-234b-49fb-8bef-3d4cbc1917f3-1746045521402.jpeg?fit=scale-down&width=60)

Lauren Oliphant

![Deep Research in action](https://cdn.gradual.com/images/https://d2xo500swnpgl1.cloudfront.net/uploads/oaiacademy/cover-image-gov-6--f5c918bc-9e73-4cf1-b000-f1f64cad0d41-1761961647331.jpeg?fit=scale-down&width=1200)

Across government, healthcare teams are tasked with making decisions in a rapidly shifting policy landscape.

The work is critical—but the research process is often slow, fragmented, and difficult to scale across federal and state priorities.

In this short demo, Lauren Oliphant, Solutions Engineer at OpenAI for Government, shows how Deep Research can help:

• Pull together recent data and analysis from high-quality sources
• Surface cost drivers, demographic trends, and policy implications
• Generate clear, structured briefings with citations and visualizations

The result: faster insight development, clearer decision support, and more time for mission-focused work—while human experts retain full control over interpretation and approval.

See how your agency could modernize its approach to policy research.

Watch the demo and learn how to get started with Deep Research.
