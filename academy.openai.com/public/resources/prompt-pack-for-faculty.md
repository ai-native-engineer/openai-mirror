# Prompt Pack for Faculty

This page provides faculty with ready-to-use prompt examples to maximize the benefits of ChatGPT Edu in teaching and research.

## Sample prompts to make the most of ChatGPT Edu

### Dynamic Case Study Development

“Generate a comprehensive case study on [emerging issue/technology], highlighting key trends and recent real-world data (from the past 6 months). Structure it for graduate-level students, including background information, challenges, and open-ended discussion questions.”

### Automated Research Grant Proposal Drafting

“Create a research grant proposal focusing on [topic]. Align it with [funding agency] guidelines (linked) and ensure the proposal includes a robust literature review, detailed methods section, and anticipated impact. Emphasize how our institution’s existing strengths support this project.”

### **Cross-Disciplinary Research Collaboration Facilitation**

“Develop a research framework integrating [discipline A] and [discipline B]. Identify common objectives, potential methodological overlaps, and areas where both fields can benefit. Provide an outline with clear goals and recommended collaboration strategies.”

### Real-Time Debate and Discussion Moderation

“Moderate a debate on [controversial topic]. Provide unbiased summaries of each side’s arguments, highlight logical fallacies or gaps, and introduce provocative questions to deepen the discourse.”

### Customized Student Feedback Analysis

“Review feedback from my [course name] for the past semester (attached). Summarize common themes, note any frequently mentioned concerns, and propose at least three actionable changes to improve the course next term."

### Personalized Learning Pathway Creation

“Build a two-track learning pathway for [subject/topic]. The first track should address fundamental concepts for beginners, while the second offers advanced challenges and research opportunities. Suggest reading materials, practical exercises, and evaluation methods.”

### Crisis Management & Response Simulation

“Develop an interactive classroom simulation on responding to a significant public health crisis. Outline scenario stages, key inflection points, and roles for students. Provide likely outcomes if certain decisions are made—both best and worst-case scenarios.”

### Research Data Visualization and Interpretation

“Using the data set [link to CSV or spreadsheet], generate clear bar charts and scatter plots showing significant correlations. Provide annotated explanations so students can easily grasp the key insights and interpret the data with minimal guidance.”
