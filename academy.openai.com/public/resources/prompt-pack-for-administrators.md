# Prompt Pack for Administrators

This page provides administrators and staff with ready-to-use prompt examples designed to maximize the benefits of ChatGPT Edu for administrative tasks, communication, planning, and daily operations.

## Sample prompts to make the most of ChatGPT Edu

### Student Enrollment Analytics and Forecasting

"Analyze our enrollment data from the past five years [linked] and generate a forecast for the upcoming academic year. Identify key trends, influencing factors, and any potential anomalies. Suggest targeted strategies for optimizing resource allocation, including recommendations for specific departments or programs. Ensure the analysis accounts for external factors such as demographic shifts or economic changes."

### Onboarding Checklist for New Hires

"Develop a comprehensive onboarding checklist for new faculty members, ensuring alignment with institutional policies and best practices. Use the resources below, and include key tasks and milestones, such as orientation sessions, training on institutional tools, and compliance requirements. Break down the checklist into pre-arrival, first week, and first month categories. Ensure the checklist is clear, actionable, and tailored to both in-person and remote onboarding scenarios. [Add data]"

### Event Planning and Coordination

“Help plan our upcoming university conference. Create a detailed schedule, assign tasks to team members, and suggest communication strategies for keeping everyone informed. [Bullet points for what needs to be included in the event]."

### Financial Aid Optimization

"Analyze our financial aid distribution data from the past [specific time period] and identify trends and opportunities to optimize funding allocation for maximum student support. Highlight any gaps in distribution, such as underrepresented groups or unmet financial needs. Provide specific, data-driven recommendations to address these gaps and enhance overall student access to financial aid. Include strategies for ongoing monitoring. [Add data] [Add specifics]."

### Crisis Response Planning

"Develop a crisis response plan for a natural disaster at [Your Campus Name]. Outline immediate steps like evacuation and emergency communication with students and staff. Include strategies for long-term recovery, focusing on rebuilding and mental health support. Tailor the plan to address specific risks, such as [common natural disasters in the area], and ensure alignment with local safety regulations. [Add data]"

### Alumni Engagement Strategy Development

Create an alumni engagement strategy for [Your Institution Name] that features personalized communication plans and tailored event suggestions. Base the strategy on alumni interests, past involvement, and engagement data. Include recommendations for targeted outreach, digital and in-person events, and ongoing relationship building to maximize alumni participation and support. [Add data]"

### Curriculum Development Support

"Analyze our current curriculum at [Your Institution Name] and provide recommendations for new courses or revisions. Base your suggestions on student feedback, industry trends, and emerging skills needed in the field. Identify any gaps in the curriculum and recommend updates to align with accreditation standards and future workforce demands. Ensure the recommendations are actionable and tailored to our institution's strengths and goals. [Upload curriculum, more details]"
