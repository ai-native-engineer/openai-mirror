## Reclaiming time and reducing stress with ChatGPT

![AI @ Home](https://cdn.gradual.com/images/https://d2xo500swnpgl1.cloudfront.net/uploads/oaiacademy/Academy-content-covers-6--e32c1240-6b58-47d4-a608-2315fb4fc1ef-1764773539790.jpeg?fit=scale-down&width=1200)

Many people are juggling work, caregiving, school, and household responsibilities with very little time or support. OpenAI Academy and Goodwill Keystone are partnering to explore how AI can ease that load and create more time for rest, planning, and opportunity at home.

OpenAI Academy’s mission is to help people use AI to solve hard, real‑world problems and open up new paths for learning and economic mobility. Goodwill Keystone brings deep experience working with job seekers and families who are balancing tight budgets, family obligations, and complex life transitions.

This training, **AI at Home**, is a practical session that shows how ChatGPT can support everyday tasks like weekly planning, meal prep, school communication, self‑care, home projects, and understanding confusing paperwork.

## Try the prompts from this session

Use these prompts to practice what you saw in the training. Edit the details in brackets to fit your life, then open them in ChatGPT.

#### 1. Plan a realistic weekly rhythm

**What this prompt does**
Helps you turn a busy week of work, childcare, and chores into a simple weekly plan that also protects time for rest.

**Sample prompt**

> Help me map out my weekly tasks to include work, childcare, chores, and protected time for rest. I work 8am-4pm Monday–Friday, do school pickup at 3pm, and want at least two 30-minute breaks for myself.

﻿[Try it in ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=Help%20me%20map%20out%20my%20weekly%20tasks%20to%20include%20work%2C%20childcare%2C%20chores%2C%20and%20protected%20time%20for%20rest.%20I%20work%208am-4pm%20Monday-Friday%2C%20do%20school%20pickup%20at%203pm%2C%20and%20want%20at%20least%20two%2030-minute%20breaks%20for%20myself.)﻿

#### 2. Create a budget-friendly meal plan

**What this prompt does**
Builds a one-week menu and organized shopping list that fits a tight grocery budget and your family’s preferences.

**Sample prompt**

> Plan meals for a family of three for one week with a $100 grocery budget. We like rice, beans, chicken, and vegetables, and need to avoid peanuts. Create a menu plus a shopping list grouped by store section.

﻿[**Try it in ChatGPT**](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=Plan%20meals%20for%20a%20family%20of%20three%20for%20one%20week%20with%20a%20%24100%20grocery%20budget.%20We%20like%20rice%2C%20beans%2C%20chicken%2C%20and%20vegetables%2C%20and%20need%20to%20avoid%20peanuts.%20Create%20a%20menu%20plus%20a%20shopping%20list%20grouped%20by%20store%20section.)﻿

#### 3. Draft a kind, clear email to a teacher

**What this prompt does**
Helps you write a respectful, short email to a teacher in a moment that might feel stressful.

**Sample prompt**

> Help me write a respectful email to my child's teacher about missing an assignment because of a family emergency. Keep it under 150 words and kind in tone.

﻿[**Try it in ChatGPT**](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=Help%20me%20write%20a%20respectful%20email%20to%20my%20child%27s%20teacher%20about%20missing%20an%20assignment%20because%20of%20a%20family%20emergency.%20Keep%20it%20under%20150%20words%20and%20kind%20in%20tone.)﻿

#### 4. Design a quick home movement routine

**What this prompt does**
Creates a simple, low-impact workout you can do in a small space with no special equipment.

**Sample prompt**

> Create a simple, low-impact workout I can do in my living room in 30 minutes. My shoulders are a bit tense from driving all day, and I don't have any special equipment.

﻿[**Try it in ChatGPT**](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=Create%20a%20simple%2C%20low-impact%20workout%20I%20can%20do%20in%20my%20living%20room%20in%2030%20minutes.%20My%20shoulders%20are%20a%20bit%20tense%20from%20driving%20all%20day%2C%20and%20I%20don%27t%20have%20any%20special%20equipment.)﻿

#### 5. Plan a low-maintenance garden

**What this prompt does**
Suggests plants and a simple plan for a backyard garden that fits your climate and doesn’t require a lot of maintenance.

**Sample prompt**

> Help me create a low-maintenance garden for my backyard. I live in Central Pennsylvania and I love flowers.

﻿[**Try it in ChatGPT**](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=Help%20me%20create%20a%20low-maintenance%20garden%20for%20my%20backyard.%20I%20live%20in%20Central%20Pennsylvania%20and%20I%20love%20flowers.)﻿

#### 6. Understand a confusing form or letter

**What this prompt does**
Turns a complicated form or letter (for example, from an insurance company) into a plain-language explanation with next steps.

**Sample prompt**

> Can you help me understand this form from my insurance company in simple language and if I need to take any action? Please remind me you are not a lawyer or financial adviser and that I should verify details with the insurer.

﻿[**Try it in ChatGPT**](https://chatgpt.com/?prompt=Can%20you%20help%20me%20understand%20this%20form%20from%20my%20insurance%20company%20in%20simple%20language%20and%20if%20I%20need%20to%20take%20any%20action%3F%20Please%20remind%20me%20you%20are%20not%20a%20lawyer%20or%20financial%20adviser%20and%20that%20I%20should%20verify%20details%20with%20the%20insurer.)﻿
