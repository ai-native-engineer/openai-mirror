# Welcome to OpenAI for Work Academy Admins Hub!

As a ChatGPT admin, you play a critical role in helping your organization unlock the value of generative AI. A smooth rollout starts with you—ensuring employees can access ChatGPT securely, confidently, and in alignment with your company’s goals. We see a lot of different people in the Admin role, so we have resources for everyone.

## **For IT & Technical Admins**

These people generally manage the **technical infrastructure** that powers a secure, seamless workspace experience. Typical responsibilities include:

* Configuring **SSO and SCIM** for secure login and user provisioning

* Managing **workspace settings** (file upload, data retention, custom GPT access)

#### **>> Start here:** [Setting up your workspace](https://academy.openai.com/home/clubs/administrators-6o6xf/tags/workspace-setup-687a6d0688989b248151f86a)﻿

## **For Program & Rollout Leads**

These admins focus on **change management and adoption**. They lead the rollout, drive employee enablement, and connect ChatGPT usage to business outcomes. Typical responsibilities include:

* Designing a **rollout plan** tied to company goals

* Driving **user enablement** and building a **champions network**

* Measuring **ROI and impact stories** to share with leadership

#### **>> Start here:** [Launching ChatGPT to your team](https://academy.openai.com/home/clubs/administrators-6o6xf/tags/launch-6877d4ea996c9ce28e11878f)﻿

## Additional communities and learning paths

As an admin, you also have access to our extended enablement ecosystem:

* **Users Community** – For employees learning how to use ChatGPT effectively in their roles.

* **Champions Community** – For leaders driving AI change across the organization.

* **Builders Community** – For technical users exploring advanced workflows, including building on the API
