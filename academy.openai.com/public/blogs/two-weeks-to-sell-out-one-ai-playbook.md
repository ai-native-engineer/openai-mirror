## How Thai media icon Woody Milintachinda used GPT-5 to plan, package content, and fill seats fast

![Two weeks to sell out, one AI playbook](https://cdn.gradual.com/images/https://d2xo500swnpgl1.cloudfront.net/uploads/oaiacademy/woody-0adcdfc4-476d-4942-8f8c-3becef9c68fa-1766068989906.jpeg?fit=scale-down&width=1200)

Thai media icon [Woody Milintachinda](https://www.linkedin.com/in/woodytalk/) had just two weeks left to sell out his upcoming leadership summit in Bangkok. With the clock ticking, he turned to GPT-5 for a plan.
He gave it a simple prompt, and the model – without needing detailed instructions – scanned his website, social channels, and other materials, then returned a comprehensive, step-by-step strategy to fill every seat. “I kept scrolling for almost 30 seconds until the end, thinking, Wow,” Woody recalls. “It felt like it had done all the homework. I could just send it to my partners and team, and they’d say, ‘There are solutions here.’ It gave them hope — and the confidence to use GPT to find answers that make sense.”
Within hours, Woody was using GPT-5 in new ways. Instead of rewriting the script for his next podcast episode, he asked the model to identify the show’s highlights, break them into shorts, and analyze what was trending on social media in Thailand. The result was a set of concise, viral-ready scripts — complete with captions — that his team could film, edit, and post immediately.
One of Thailand’s most influential TV hosts and producers, Woody has built a career creating shows, festivals, and cultural moments recognized across Asia. Now, with GPT-5, he’s adding AI-powered planning and content creation to his toolkit — helping him move from idea to execution in record time.
“GPT-5 gives us solutions we can act on right away,” Woody says. “It’s like having a partner who’s always ready with the next great idea.”
