### AI Reflection Form for Higher Education

The AI Reflection Form is a quick, high-impact tool designed to help students document and reflect on their use of AI while completing assignments. AI reflection forms are now being used across disciplines to surface student thinking, normalize intentional AI use, and foster skills students will need in future workplaces. It takes just a few minutes to complete and gives educators valuable insight into when and how AI supports learning, and where it might fall short.
