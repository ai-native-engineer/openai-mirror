### Use cases for Codex by department

Share these with non-dev teams to show them how Codex can support real work across departments like Sales, Marketing, Finance, HR, Legal, Data Science, Business Operations, and executive workflows. The goal is to help Champions and teams move from abstract interest in agentic AI to more concrete ideas they can recognize, discuss, and adapt inside their own organizations.
Each one-pager highlights examples of how Codex can help with common work patterns like research, synthesis, workflow execution, content generation, analysis, coordination, and decision support. Use this collection to spark conversations with stakeholders, identify relevant starting points for different teams, and make it easier to connect Codex to everyday work.

Over time, these examples build credibility, encourage adoption, and make successful workflows easier for other teams to understand and repeat.

### Run a prompt challenge

This guide helps Activators run a focused prompt challenge that gives people a practical reason to try ChatGPT and surfaces examples the broader team can test and reuse.
You’ll learn how to choose a relevant challenge, make participation easy, curate the strongest submissions, and build on the results so the challenge creates more than short-term activity. Leaders can support the challenge by connecting it to a business priority, creating space to participate, and reinforcing when the strongest examples should be reused.
