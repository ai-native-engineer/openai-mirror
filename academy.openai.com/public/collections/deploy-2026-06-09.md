### Build and grow a network of local AI Activators

This guide helps Leaders build and strengthen a network of Local Activators: Champions embedded in teams, functions, business units, or regions who help colleagues adopt validated AI workflows and share what works.
The goal is not simply to create an active network. It is to help validated workflows spread more quickly, make local support easier to access, surface blockers earlier, and reduce dependence on one central Leader to drive adoption.

### Capture and share use cases and impact

This guide helps Champions move from creating AI tools and testing workflows to proving their value.
By identifying, documenting, and sharing validated use cases, Champions can show where AI is making work faster, clearer, more consistent, or easier to complete. Over time, these examples build credibility, encourage adoption, and make successful workflows easier for other teams to understand and repeat.
