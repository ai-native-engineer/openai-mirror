# Reporting Content in ChatGPT and OpenAI Platforms

Learn how to report content across conversations, shared links, GPTs, and forums that may violate OpenAI’s Terms of Use or applicable laws.

Updated: 3 days ago

You can report conversations, shared links, responses, GPTs, and forum posts that may violate OpenAI’s [Terms of Use](https://openai.com/policies/row-terms-of-use/) or applicable laws via our [content reporting webform](https://openai.com/form/report-content/).  
  
Reported domains and other content may be reviewed by OpenAI’s Model Quality team, which may apply filters or other mitigations to help prevent ChatGPT from relying on unreliable sources in future responses.  
  
You may also be able to make a report in-product, see below for how to report across platforms.

# Conversations with ChatGPT

You can report a ChatGPT conversation directly from the message in question on both mobile (iOS and Android) and web (chatgpt.com).

1. Tap thumbs down (👎) under a ChatGPT message.
2. Tap Select an issue.
3. Select “Safety or Legal concern”.
4. Follow the prompts to submit your feedback.

# Shared Links

## iOS (App)

1. Tap the three-dot menu (...) at the top-left of the screen.
2. Tap Report.
3. Follow the prompts to submit your feedback.

## Android (App)

1. Open a Shared Link Scroll to the bottom of the shared link page.
2. Scroll to the top of the conversation.
3. Click 'Report conversation' and follow the prompts.

## Web (chat.openai.com/share/...)

1. Open a Shared Link.
2. Scroll to the top of the conversation.
3. Click ‘Report conversation’ and follow the prompts.

# Conversations with GPTs

## iOS (App)

1. Click on a GPT and start a conversation.
2. Tap ‘GPT Name’ on the top-middle of the screen.
3. Tap Report.
4. Tap 'Report conversation' and follow the prompts.

## Android (App)

1. Click on a GPT and start a conversation.
2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) at the top-right.
3. Select ‘Report’ then ‘Conversation’ and follow the prompts.

## Web (chat.openai.com)

1. Start a conversation with a GPT.
2. Click on the (...) button at the top right of your screen.
3. Click Report and follow the prompts.

# GPTs

Note that if a GPT infringes on your trademark (such as your brand name or logo), please fill out our [trademark dispute form](https://openai.com/form/trademark-disputes) with the required information instead.

## iOS (App)

1. Click on a GPT.
2. Tap ‘GPT Name’ on the top-middle of the screen.
3. Tap Report.
4. Follow the prompts to submit your feedback.

## Android (App)

1. Click on a GPT.
2. Tap ‘GPT Name’ on the top-middle of the screen.
3. Tap Report.
4. Follow the prompts to submit your feedback.

*(Note: if you’ve already started a conversation with the GPT, you’ll click ‘Report’ then ‘GPT’ to report the GPT as a whole and not your specific conversation)*

## Web (chat.openai.com)

1. Click on a GPT from the GPT Store, or from an external link.
2. Click the GPT’s name at the top left of your screen
3. Click ‘Report GPT’ and follow the prompts

## Ads content

### Web

To report an ad in ChatGPT:

1. On the ad, click **⋯** (top right).
2. Select **Report this ad**.
3. In the pop-up, choose why you’re reporting it.
4. (Optional) Provide additional details in the text box.
5. Click **Submit**. You can submit without entering any additional details.

# OpenAI Forums

## OpenAI Community Forum

1. Go to [openai.com/form/report-content](https://openai.com/form/report-content/).
2. Submit your report with a link to the content in question.

📎 Tip: Copy the URL of the message directly from your browser’s address bar.

## OpenAI Developer Forum

1. Navigate to [openai.com/form/report-content](https://openai.com/form/report-content/), or click **“Report illegal content”** from the **footer** of any thread.
2. To report a specific comment:

   * Click the **link icon (🔗)** on the bottom-right of the post or comment.
   * Copy that URL and paste it in the report form.
