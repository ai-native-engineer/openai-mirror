# Managing credits and spend controls in ChatGPT Business

Learn when ChatGPT Business needs credits, how to add credits and auto-top-up, and how to review usage analytics and spend controls for Codex.

Updated: 1 hour ago

# Overview

***Note:*** *Starting June 24, 2026, Codex seats will no longer be available to new ChatGPT Business plans, or to Business workspaces that never added a Codex seat prior to June 24 2026. Business workspaces that had added a Codex seat prior to June 24, 2026, including pending invites as of June 24 can continue to manage and add usage-based Codex seats.*

Use this article if your workspace has Codex seats, expects additional Codex usage from standard ChatGPT seats, or needs workspace-level limits and analytics. Note that Codex seats require credits for activity.  
  
If your workspace does not have enough credits for a usage-based feature, that feature may be unavailable until credits are added.  
  
Learn more about credits in our article: [Flexible pricing for Enterprise, Edu, and Business plans](/en/articles/11487671-flexible-pricing-for-the-enterprise-edu-and-business-plans)

## Add credits to a workspace

* Sign in with a [**role**](/en/articles/8542216-managing-workspaces-people-and-access-in-chatgpt-business#managing-chatgpt-business-workspaces) that is allowed to manage workspace billing or credits.
* Go to **Workspace settings →** [**Billing**](https://chatgpt.com/admin/billing).
* Select the option to add credits.
* Review the payment method, credit amount, and note that credits are valid for 12 months.
* Confirm the purchase.

If you add the first Codex user to a workspace that previously had only standard ChatGPT seats, the product can trigger a credits flow using the saved payment method.

## Use automatic reload

Automatic reload helps prevent interruptions when a workspace uses credits for Codex activity. You can set a **Minimum balance** so ChatGPT automatically charges your payment method on file to add credits when your balance goes below that amount and tops up to your **Target balance**. If your workspace balance is already below the **Minimum balance** when you turn on automatic reload, ChatGPT may charge the payment method on file right away to add credits, up to the **Target balance** and subject to any **Monthly recharge limit**. To cap automatic reload purchases, enter a **Monthly recharge limit**; leaving this field blank allows unlimited automatic reload purchases each month.

* Go to **Workspace settings → Billing**.
* Turn on **Automatic reload**.
* Review the **Minimum balance**, **Target balance**, **Monthly recharge limit**, and payment method.
* Click **Turn on** to enable the change.

## Manage spend controls

You can manage monthly credit usage limits by seat type or by specific user, to align with your workspace needs. For example, you can set a higher credit limit (or no limit) for Codex seats, while keeping limits for ChatGPT seats. You can also set limits by user - note that this value will override a prior seat-specific limit.  
  
By default, all seats and users have no limits specified.

* Go to [**Workspace settings → Billing**](https://chatgpt.com/admin/billing).
* Identify the seat type you want to add a limit for, and click **Add** to set a credit maximum.
* You can also set a **Per user override**. Click **Manage** to select limits per user, and then **Add** to set the limit.

### Privacy

Spend controls are operational tools. They do not replace the privacy and chat-visibility rules for the workspace.  
  
A user's private chat history is still separate unless that user chooses to share a specific chat, GPT, or other resource.
