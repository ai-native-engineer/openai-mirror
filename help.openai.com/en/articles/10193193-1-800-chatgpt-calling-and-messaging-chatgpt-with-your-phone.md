# 1-800-ChatGPT - Calling and Messaging ChatGPT with your phone

Updated: 10 days ago

By communicating with 1-800-ChatGPT, you agree to our [Terms of Use](https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use/) and have read our [Privacy Policy](https://privacy.openai.com/policies). Remember, ChatGPT can make mistakes, so please check important info. This service is for 13+.

## What is 1-800-ChatGPT?

1-800-ChatGPT is an experimental feature that enables wider access to ChatGPT. You can interact with ChatGPT via phone calls at 1-800-CHATGPT without needing an account.  
  
You can start a conversation by calling 1-800-CHATGPT (1-800-242-8478) from a U.S. or Canadian number from [supported countries](/en/articles/7947663-chatgpt-supported-countries). Standard carrier fees may apply.  
  
  
**Important: WhatsApp integration has been deprecated and is no longer available as of January 15, 2026.**

### FAQ

**What if I need help?**  
  
Contact OpenAI Support by clicking on the chat icon on the bottom-right of this page. We're happy to help!

## Can I unlink my phone number from my OpenAI account?

We do not currently allow users to unlink phone numbers from OpenAI accounts.

## What usage limits apply when I call 1-800-ChatGPT?

You can talk to 1-800-ChatGPT for 30 minutes per month for free (US and CA only). We may adjust usage limits based on capacity if needed.  
  
We provide a notice as you approach the limit and inform you when the limit has been reached.  
  
You can continue chatting with ChatGPT by downloading the ChatGPT app at [chatgpt.com/download](http://chatgpt.com/download) or going to chatgpt.com.

## Do you have any tips for preventing interruptions with voice conversations?

For the best experience:

* Use 1-800-ChatGPT in a quiet environment.
* Background noise may cause unintended responses; we recommend enabling noise isolation features on your device.
* On iPhones, enabling *Voice Isolation* mode via *Control Panel > Mic Mode > Voice Isolation* can help to avoid unintentional interruptions
* Using headphones or earphones can help prevent interruptions.

## Do you retain conversations from 1-800-ChatGPT?

We do store and may review your calls and transcripts of calls with 1-800-ChatGPT for a limited period of time for safety and abuse prevention purposes. [Learn more](/en/articles/7039943-data-usage-for-consumer-services-faq#h_fdfcbe9ebe) about how we may review your content for safety.

## How do I export my data from 1-800-ChatGPT or request for data to be deleted?

Your conversations with 1-800-ChatGPT are associated with the phone number you used to call 1-800-ChatGPT. For information about how to access or delete your data, you can review our [Privacy Policy](https://privacy.openai.com/policies).

## Why is 1-800-ChatGPT telling me it can’t help me or being unresponsive to my prompts?

There are multiple reasons 1-800-ChatGPT may not respond to your prompts. This is the first time we are enabling access to ChatGPT through phone calls. We expect to continue improving 1-800-ChatGPT as we better understand its usage.  
  
You may also not be getting a response to your prompt as a result of our safety measures. If it seems like your prompt is in line with our Usage Policies, then please send us feedback at [help.openai.com](https://help.openai.com/en/) or click on the widget in the bottom right hand corner of this page. To report potential policy violations or illegal content, you can do so at our Report Content [webform](https://openai.com/form/report-content/) and select ChatGPT as the product.

## My number has been blocked, can you please unblock my number?

We’re sorry to hear that your number has been blocked from accessing 1-800-ChatGPT. This may occur if we believe your usage violates our [Terms of Use](https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use/) or if you are calling from an unsupported region. If you believe this was done in error, please submit a request by clicking on the help widget in the bottom right hand corner of this page.
