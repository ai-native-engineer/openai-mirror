# ChatGPT iOS app - iPad drag & drop

How "drag & drop" functionality works in the ChatGPT iOS app for iPad

Updated: 10 days ago

While using the ChatGPT iOS app on an iPad you can press and hold on a generated response then drag the entire thing into another application.  
  
On a iPad you can use this to move a generated response from the ChatGPT iOS app via drag & drop into a notes app or a spreadsheet.
