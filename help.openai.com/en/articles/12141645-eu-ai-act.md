# EU AI Act

This article sets out helpful information on our compliance with the EU AI Act.

Updated: 3 days ago

OpenAI is committed to developing models that comply with applicable EU AI Act requirements and to helping our customers obtain the information they need to manage their own EU AI Act compliance. We will update this page periodically with helpful information and resources.  
  
  
**Resources**  
  
For more information on OpenAI’s overall approach to the EU AI Act and the Code of Practice for General Purpose AI Models, please see our blog post, [A Primer on the EU AI Act](https://openai.com/global-affairs/a-primer-on-the-eu-ai-act/).  
  
For more information about the AI Act’s prohibited practices, please see our Help Center article, [Complying with the EU AI Act’s Prohibited Practices Requirements](/en/articles/10658393-complying-with-the-eu-ai-acts-prohibited-practices-requirements).  
  
For more information about how we handle safety, please see our [Safety Approach](https://openai.com/safety/), where you will find our System Cards and our [Preparedness Framework](https://openai.com/index/updating-our-preparedness-framework/). We also share public information about our safety evaluation results in our [Safety Evals Hub](https://openai.com/safety/evaluations-hub/).  
  
  
**Contact**  
  
If you have general inquiries related to the AI Act, please contact us at: [euaiact-inquiries@openai.com](mailto:euaiact-inquiries@openai.com).
