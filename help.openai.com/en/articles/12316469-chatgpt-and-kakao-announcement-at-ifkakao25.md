# ChatGPT and Kakao - Announcement at if(kakao)25

Updated: 10 days ago

Today we announced a partnership with Kakao to bring ChatGPT experiences to KakaoTalk. This was a preview of features that will be coming soon.

## What changed today

Nothing changes for your ChatGPT account, plan, or data settings. There is no beta, waitlist, or account linking right now.

# FAQ

## Can I use ChatGPT in KakaoTalk now?

Not yet. Today’s news is an announcement. There’s nothing new to enable in KakaoTalk.

## Is there a beta or waitlist?

Not at this time. We’ll share sign-up information if that changes.

## Will this work outside Korea?

The ChatGPT experience in KakaoTalk will be available in the **Korean version of KakaoTalk**. You can continue using **ChatGPT on the web and mobile apps** wherever they’re supported. The **same OpenAI account** works in both.

## Does this change how my data is handled?

No. There are no changes to your ChatGPT data or privacy settings today. We’ll document data flow and controls ahead of launch.
