# Why Did I Receive a Warning About My Account?

Updated: 3 days ago

If you’ve received a warning related to your OpenAI account or usage, you might be wondering why it happened and what it means for your future use of our services. We issue warnings to ensure that all users comply with our [Usage Policies](https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies/) and [Terms of Service](https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use/), helping to maintain a safe and responsible environment.

## Why Did I Receive a Warning?

Warnings are typically issued when activity associated with your account raises concerns regarding policy compliance. Common reasons include:

## 1. Content Violations

Our systems may detect prompts or outputs that conflict with our usage guidelines. Examples include:

* Generating harmful or unsafe content.
* Content that violates our policies, such as sexual content involving minors or harassment.

## 2. Attempts to Bypass Safety Systems

Warnings may be issued if you attempt to:

* Circumvent built-in safety filters or content restrictions.
* Exploit vulnerabilities in the platform to generate disallowed content.

## 3. Misuse of the API or Platform

Violations can include:

* Using services for automated spam, phishing, or other deceptive activities.
* Unauthorized sharing or misuse of API keys.
* Excessive or abnormal usage patterns that raise compliance concerns.

## What Does This Warning Mean?

A warning is a notice, not an immediate suspension. It’s an opportunity to review and adjust your usage to ensure compliance. Continued violations after a warning may lead to further restrictions or permanent account deactivation.

## Next Steps

## 1. Review Our Policies

Familiarize yourself with our [Usage Policies](https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies/) and [Terms of Service](https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use/) to understand what content and activities are permitted.

## 2. Monitor and Adjust Your Usage

* Avoid prompts that may result in disallowed content.
* Use tools like the [Moderations endpoint](https://cookbook.openai.com/examples/how_to_use_moderation) to detect and prevent problematic requests.

## How to Prevent Future Warnings

* Use clear and policy-compliant prompts.
* Stay updated on any changes to our [Usage Policies](https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies/).
* Regularly review your account activity and ensure only authorized users have access.

## Can I Appeal a Warning?

If you believe your account received the warning in error, please submit your appeal using the link in the email notification you received. This allows us to route your request accurately and review it more efficiently.  
  
If you did not receive an email notification but have lost access to your account, please reach out via the Help Centre chat for support.  
  
Please include the following details:

* User ID and Org ID that you're filing an appeal for
* Any relevant context or details regarding your usage
* Date range of suspected compromise, if relevant
* Card details if you noticed any unauthorized charges due to unrecognized usage:

  + Date(s) of the charge(s)
  + Amount(s) of the charge(s)
  + Last 4 digits of the card charged
  + Brand of the Card (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)
  + Name of the issuing bank
  + Billing Zip Code
* Information about steps you've taken to resolve any issues.

If you can’t access that email, please file an appeal via our intake form: <https://openai.com/form/appeal/>.  
  
Our team will review your request and respond as promptly as possible.
