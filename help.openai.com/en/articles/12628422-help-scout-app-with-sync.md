# Help Scout — app with sync

Updated: 10 days ago

## Overview

The Help Scout app with sync lets ChatGPT securely access your team’s Help Scout conversations and threads, customers and mailboxes to answer questions. You can connect, disconnect, or update the connection at any time. Once enabled, ChatGPT will automatically reference content in Help Scout when relevant.  
  
Find this app in the ChatGPT [app directory](https://chatgpt.com/apps), or from Settings > Apps.  
  
  
**Type:** app with sync  
  
  
**Where you can use it:** Chat, deep research  
  
  
[**Learn more about setting up apps in ChatGPT.**](/en/articles/11487775)

* “Summarize Help Scout conversations from the past day”

## Capabilities and permissions

### What it can access

* Conversations and threads
* Customers and mailboxes
* Tags, folders, status, assignees

### Not indexed

* Large binary attachments (e.g., videos), embedded media, and unsupported file types
* Items outside your permission scope
* System-generated or ephemeral metadata not exposed via the API

### Permissions (scope)

* read

### Known limitations

* Initial sync time depends on data volume (minutes to hours)
* API rate limits and provider throttling may delay syncs
* Permission or configuration changes are applied on the next sync cycle
* Some attachment binaries or non-text content may be excluded
