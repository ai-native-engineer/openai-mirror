# Can I access my ChatGPT subscription from another device?

Updated: 10 days ago

Yes, you can access your subscription on another device by logging in with the same OpenAI account that made the original purchase. Please note that the account must be active and not deleted.  
  
If you purchased your subscription via iOS or Android, you'll need to manage your subscription via [Apple subscriptions](/en/articles/7905690-how-do-i-cancel-my-apple-subscription-for-chatgpt-plus-in-the-chatgpt-ios-app) or [Google Play subscriptions](/en/articles/8258076-how-to-cancel-a-plus-subscription-in-the-chatgpt-android-app).
