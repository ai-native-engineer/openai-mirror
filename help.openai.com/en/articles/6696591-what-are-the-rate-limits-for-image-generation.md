# What are the rate limits for Image Generation?

Learn how to can check your rate limit for our image generation models, including GPT Image API and DALL·E API.

Updated: 10 days ago

The default rate limit for our image generation APIs depends on which model you are using along with your usage tier. For more details and specifics, we recommend checking out the respective model page for each image model:

* [GPT Image](https://platform.openai.com/docs/models/gpt-image-1)
* [DALL·E 3](https://platform.openai.com/docs/models/dall-e-3)
* [DALL·E 2](https://platform.openai.com/docs/models/dall-e-2)

To get started with image generation in the API, head to our [image generation developer guide](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/images).

Learn more in our [rate limits guide](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/rate-limits/usage-tiers). You can also check the specific limits for your account in your [limits page](https://platform.openai.com/account/limits).
