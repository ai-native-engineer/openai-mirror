# How can I change my invoice date?

Learn how billing and invoicing work for ChatGPT and API usage.

Updated: 2 days ago

## Can I change my invoice date?

At this time, OpenAI does **not support changes to invoice dates**. All billing cycles and invoice generation schedules are fixed based on your subscription type.  
  
---

## ChatGPT Subscriptions

* Billing occurs on a **monthly basis** from the date you subscribed.
* For example, if you subscribed on the 12th of the month, you will be billed again on the 12th of each following month.
* Invoice dates align with this recurring schedule and cannot be modified.

---

## API Usage (Pay-as-you-go)

* Invoices are issued **monthly**, covering the **previous calendar month's** usage.
* Example: An invoice for usage during March will be generated and issued in April.
* All API billing is postpaid and calculated automatically.
