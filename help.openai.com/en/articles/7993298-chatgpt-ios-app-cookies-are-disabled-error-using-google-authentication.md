# ChatGPT iOS app "Cookies are disabled" error using Google authentication

Updated: 10 days ago

If you have [cookies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTTP_cookie) blocked then you won't be able to login.  
  
If you see a "Cookies are disabled" error signing in with Google authentication:

![Google sign-in error message stating Cookies are disabled](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-4167bbbf50f3f5c8ec6077f7/99f366b94d2029ae51483a5185f23699/Screenshot_2023-06-08_at_2.12.50_PM.png)

Then you will need to [disable the "Block cookies" setting in Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201265):

![iPhone Safari settings with Block All Cookies enabled](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-6272c24426a25b29c6baabbc/a54a1827a4a05eb8a14cd70562ebf6f5/Screenshot_2023-06-05_at_1.44.24_PM.png)

Once cookies are enabled you should be able to sign in to resolve the "Cookies are disabled" error.
