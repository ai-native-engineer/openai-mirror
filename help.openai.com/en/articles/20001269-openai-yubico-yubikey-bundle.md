# OpenAI + Yubico YubiKey bundle

Learn about the YubiKey bundle for eligible OpenAI users.

Updated: 2 days ago

# Overview

OpenAI is committed to helping users access best-in-class account protection. One of the strongest forms of protection against phishing and unauthorized account access is a hardware security key, such as a YubiKey. To make hardware security keys more accessible, OpenAI has partnered with Yubico to offer eligible OpenAI users access to a tailored bundle of YubiKeys at preferred pricing.  
  
For users who choose to use hardware security keys for sign-in, ChatGPT supports any FIDO-compatible security key, including the YubiKeys included in this bundle.

# What is included

The bundle includes two YubiKeys: a YubiKey C Nano designed to stay in your laptop for low-friction everyday authentication, and a YubiKey C NFC that gives you a backup key that is also convenient across desktop and mobile devices. This bundle is set up without OTP support, reducing fallback paths and helping strengthen the overall sign-in experience. These keys support passkeys and multiple authentication protocols, so they can be used with many other apps and services.  
  
The Yubico checkout page shows the current bundle details, pricing, taxes, shipping options, and other purchase details before you complete your order.

# Availability

The YubiKey bundle may be available to eligible users in the US, UK, and EEA. Availability may vary by country, account eligibility, inventory, and other restrictions.  
  
You need to be signed in to your OpenAI account to verify eligibility and access the purchase experience. If the YubiKey bundle page or **Order a YubiKey** prompt is not available for your account, you can still use another compatible FIDO security key or passkey.

# Order the bundle

To order the bundle:

1. Sign in to your OpenAI account.
2. Go to the [YubiKey bundle page](https://chatgpt.com/yubikey).
3. Follow the prompts to verify eligibility.
4. Complete checkout on Yubico's website.

You may also see an **Order a YubiKey** prompt while enrolling in Advanced Account Security.  
  
When you select **Order a YubiKey** or open the **YubiKey bundle** page, you are redirected to Yubico's website to complete the purchase. Any information you provide to Yubico during checkout is subject to Yubico's terms and privacy policy.  
  
YubiKey purchases are completed through Yubico. For order status, shipping, returns, refunds, payment issues, ecommerce issues, or hardware support, contact [Yubico Support](https://support.yubico.com/s/requests).

# Use YubiKeys with Advanced Account Security

Advanced Account Security requires at least two secure sign-in methods, including one that works across devices. These can include passkeys, FIDO-compatible security keys, or a combination of both. The OpenAI + Yubico YubiKey bundle is one eligible form of secure sign-in that is compatible with this program. If you order the OpenAI + Yubico YubiKey bundle, you can use the keys with Advanced Account Security after you receive and set them up. For more information about enrolling, see: [Advanced Account Security](https://help.openai.com/articles/20001221).

# YubiKey order and hardware support

YubiKey purchases are completed through Yubico. For anything related to purchasing YubiKeys, Yubico's ecommerce site, order status, shipping, returns, refunds, payment issues, or issues with a YubiKey itself, submit a request to [Yubico Support](https://support.yubico.com/s/requests).  
  
OpenAI Support can help with OpenAI account and Advanced Account Security setup questions, including whether the **Order a YubiKey** option appears for your account and how to use a compatible security key after you receive it. OpenAI does not process YubiKey orders, returns, refunds, shipping requests, ecommerce support, or hardware support for YubiKeys.

# FAQ

## What if the bundle is not available for my account?

If the YubiKey bundle page or **Order a YubiKey** prompt is not available for your account, you can still use another compatible FIDO security key or passkey.

## Do I need a YubiKey to use Advanced Account Security?

No. A YubiKey is optional. You can use passkeys, compatible FIDO security keys, or a combination of both, as long as you meet the setup requirements.

## Can I order the bundle without enrolling in Advanced Account Security?

Yes. Eligible users can order through the offer even if they do not enroll in Advanced Account Security.

## Can OpenAI refund my YubiKey order?

No. OpenAI does not process YubiKey payments, returns, refunds, shipping requests, or ecommerce support. Refund eligibility and return handling are managed by Yubico under Yubico's terms and policies.  
  
For help with a YubiKey purchase, return, refund, or ecommerce issue, submit a request to [Yubico Support](https://support.yubico.com/s/requests).
