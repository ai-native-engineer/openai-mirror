# How can I delete or remove my payment method?

Updated: 2 days ago

For self-serve ChatGPT and/or API Platform customers, you can delete or remove your payment method on file via the appropriate billing settings page. Your account must be fully verified before you can add, update, or delete payment methods.

### Troubleshooting / FAQ

* Why the "Remove/Delete payment method" button may be unavailable
* Active subscription: if you have an active or recurring subscription, the system may prevent removing the payment method until the subscription is canceled or replaced.
* Scheduled cancellation: if you've scheduled cancellation, the payment method may remain until the cancellation takes effect.
* Past-due invoice or recent payment failure: outstanding charges can block removal until the invoice is paid or the payment issue is resolved.
* If you receive past-due or payment-failure emails
* Resolve the outstanding invoice (pay the invoice or update your payment method) using your billing settings, or follow the instructions in the email. If you can’t resolve the charge, contact Billing Support for help.
* What happens after removal
* Removing a payment method does not delete billing history or retroactively remove charges.
* If you remove the only valid payment method while you have active paid services (including API usage), billing attempts may fail and paid services can be suspended or restricted. To avoid interruption, add a replacement payment method before removing the existing one.
* Business access edge cases
* Deactivated workspaces or lack of admin/owner access can prevent managing payment methods; contact your workspace owner or admin to update billing.
* If ownership is transferring, coordinate billing changes with the new owner to ensure uninterrupted service.
* Billing records and suspected fraud
* Billing records are retained for accounting and support purposes; removing a payment method does not erase past invoices.
* If you suspect fraud or unauthorized charges, contact Support via the Help Center as soon as possible and include relevant transaction details so the team can investigate.

### ChatGPT Plus / Pro

Navigate to your [*Settings > Account*](https://chatgpt.com/#settings/Account) page and click on *Payment > Manage*:

![ChatGPT Account settings with the Payment section and Manage button highlighted](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-d0f838526f9043a4679b742a/9b8db2967830783d83454e6ef1c67010/Payment_details.png)

Under **Payment Method**, you can add or remove existing payment methods.

![Payment method list with American Express ending in 1009 and the remove X icon highlighted](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-de1157c841249dc37bd619a2/baecc692b8e194c858a162d5c30114f2/payment-method-delete.png)

### ChatGPT Business

From your [workspace settings](https://chatgpt.com/admin/billing), navigate to the *Billing > Plan* tab and click on *Manage plan > Payment method*.

![Billing page Manage plan menu opened to Payment method](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-ef823c574c0f12adc0cf08a6/2d0f91f0884e6986257416ddb11dead3/billing_payment_method.png)

Under **Payment Method**, you can add or remove existing payment methods.

![Payment method page with the remove X icon highlighted next to the default American Express card](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-1205dae71ce4ad76b1a634f3/e40ea269acbc59eac1565041f648acdd/payment-method-delete.png)

### API Organization (Self-Serve)

From your [API organization's settings](https://platform.openai.com/settings/organization/billing/payment-methods), navigate to the *Billing > Payment methods* tab. You can then add or remove an existing payment method.

![Billing Payment methods tab with a red arrow pointing to the Delete link for a saved card](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-04fe705d33fb5995f7a4a3c3/b52e6cd1c790ad7d65ceae4a511b8346/Screenshot_2025-09-22_at_12_10_47_E2_80_AFPM.png)
