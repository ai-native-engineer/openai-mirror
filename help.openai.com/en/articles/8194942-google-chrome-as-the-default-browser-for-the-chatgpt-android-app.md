# Google Chrome as the Default Browser for the ChatGPT Android App

Learn why Chrome is required for logging in to the ChatGPT Android app and how to set Chrome as your default browser.

Updated: 10 days ago

## Why is Chrome recommended for ChatGPT on Android?

The ChatGPT Android app relies on a supported browser (Chrome or Brave) to securely complete the login process. If neither browser is installed or set as default, you may encounter login errors or be unable to sign in.  
  
To ensure the best experience, we recommend setting **Google Chrome** as your default browser.

## Supported Browsers for Login

* **Google Chrome** (recommended)
* **Brave**

Other browsers may not support the login flow and could result in an error.

## How to Set Google Chrome as Your Default Browser

Follow these steps on most Android devices:

1. **Open** the **Settings** app on your Android device.
2. Tap **Apps** (or **Apps & notifications** depending on your version).
3. Tap **Default apps**.
4. Select **Browser app**.
5. Choose **Chrome** from the list.

Once set, ChatGPT and other apps will open web links using Chrome by default.  
  
  
**Tip**: If you don’t see the “Default apps” option, you may need to tap **Advanced** within the Apps section.

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

* **Not seeing Chrome as an option?**

Make sure Chrome is installed from the Google Play Store.

* **Using a custom Android skin (e.g. MIUI, One UI)?**

You may need to:

1. Go to **Settings > Apps > Manage apps**
2. Tap your current default browser (e.g., MI Browser or Samsung Internet)
3. Select **Clear defaults**
4. Then reattempt setting Chrome as default using the steps above
