# What is ChatGPT Go?

Information about our paid subscription plan, Go

Updated: 2 days ago

ChatGPT Go is a new, low-cost subscription plan that provides expanded access to ChatGPT’s most popular features at an affordable price.

***Note****: ChatGPT Go is now available in all* [*ChatGPT supported countries*](/en/articles/7947663-chatgpt-supported-countries)*.*

# What's included

Everything included in the [Free plan](/en/articles/9275245-chatgpt-free-tier-faq), and:

* *Unlimited*\* chat access to GPT-5.5 Instant\*\*: Enjoy more usage of our flagship model.
* **Extended access to image generation**: Create more images for work or play.
* **Extended access to file uploads**: Analyze and work with more documents, spreadsheets, and other files.
* **Extended access to advanced data analysis**: Use tools like Python for data exploration and problem-solving more often.
* **Longer memory for more personalized responses**: Keep conversations flowing with a larger context window.
* **Access to projects, tasks, custom GPTs, and Library**: Organize your work, track progress, create / edit custom GPTs, and save up to 4 GB in Library.
* **Access to projects, tasks, and custom GPTs**: Organize your work, track progress, and create / edit custom GPTs to build AI tools tailored to your needs.

\* Unlimited or virtually unlimited access is subject to abuse guardrails and any model-specific usage allowances. Temporary restrictions may apply to prevent misuse.  
  
We may start testing ads in ChatGPT Go in the future. Learn more about ads in our [Help Center FAQ.](/en/articles/20001047)

***Note:*** *API usage is not included and billed independently. See* [*API pricing*](https://openai.com/api/pricing) *for more details.*

# Availability

ChatGPT Go is available in all countries ChatGPT is [supported](/en/articles/7947663-chatgpt-supported-countries) in. All purchases are in USD - we offer a local currency billing in a limited set of countries (see: [Multi-currency billing](/en/articles/10421635-multi-currency-billing)). Subscription prices can be found in the [ChatGPT pricing page](https://chatgpt.com/pricing/). Note that we may adjust pricing over time as we learn from early use and gather feedback.  
  
Subscriptions are billed automatically monthly, and can be canceled anytime.

# How do I sign up?

1. Log into ChatGPT.
2. Click on your profile icon → **Upgrade Plan**.
3. Select **Try Go**.

# Usage Limits

ChatGPT Go includes higher usage limits than the Free plan for core chat and tools. Limits may vary based on system conditions to ensure a smooth experience for all users.

# Billing and Invoices

You can manage your subscription, view invoices, and cancel your plan by:

1. Log into ChatGPT.
2. Click on your profile icon → **Settings**.
3. Select **Account**.

# FAQ

## Is there an annual plan available?

Currently, we do not support annual billing or the option to pay for multiple months in advance for ChatGPT Go, Plus, or Pro subscriptions.

## What payment method can I use to pay for ChatGPT Go?

**Payment methods vary by country.** See: [Multi-currency billing](/en/articles/10421635)

## How is ChatGPT Go different from Plus?

ChatGPT Go gives you more access to our most popular features: more GPT-5.5 messages, multimodal tools like image generation and file uploads, and longer memory at an affordable price point. [ChatGPT Plus](/en/articles/6950777-what-is-chatgpt-plus) is designed for advanced users: it includes expanded access to thinking and legacy models, and advanced tools like deep research, and agent mode.

## Are reasoning models included with ChatGPT Go?

Yes, ChatGPT Go includes access to reasoning models (“thinking”). To use reasoning, select **Thinking** from the plus "**+"** menu.

![ChatGPT composer plus menu with Thinking selected](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/151Niz4VHuosR5fLk7U1w0/ee39534e6d31a7db326ceb5cdb0d452d/Screenshot_2026-01-14_at_10.28.38_AM.png)

## I’m already a ChatGPT Plus or Pro subscriber, can I switch to Go?

Yes - to manage or change your subscription, you can:

1. Log into ChatGPT.
2. Click on your profile icon → **Settings**.
3. Select **Account**.

## Can I use legacy models such as 4o with ChatGPT Go?

No. Access to legacy models is not included with the ChatGPT Go subscription.

## Will I be refunded if I switch to ChatGPT Go from Pro or Plus?

No. Your current plan will remain active until the end of your billing cycle. At that time, your account will switch to Go, and you will be charged the Go rate moving forward.

## Is access to apps included?

[Apps](/en/articles/11487775-apps-in-chatgpt) and the ChatGPT app directory are available, but select apps will require a ChatGPT Plus (or higher) subscription.

## Is access to Sora included?

No. Access to [Sora](http://sora.com) is not included. Access is available starting with the ChatGPT [Plus](/en/articles/6950777-what-is-chatgpt-plus) subscription.

## I upgraded to ChatGPT Go but I do not see it in my account

If you subscribed to ChatGPT Go through the App Store or Play Store, it may take a few minutes for the upgrade to appear in the ChatGPT app. If your subscription isn’t showing, wait a few minutes and restart the app.

## Is access to voice mode included?

Yes. [Voice mode](/en/articles/8400625-voice-mode-faq) is included with the same usage limits as the Free tier.
