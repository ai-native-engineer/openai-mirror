# How can I contact sales?

Learn how to connect with OpenAI’s Sales team for Enterprise or API needs.

Updated: last month

## ChatGPT Enterprise

Before you contact Sales:

* The sales contact form is the only supported channel—there is no public phone line or direct email for sales inquiries.
* Our Sales team may not respond to every inquiry, and our Support team cannot expedite a response or provide status updates.
* If the form will not submit, try disabling browser extensions, clearing your cookies, or using a different browser or network.
* Include your work email, company name, country/region, estimated seat count, ideal timeline, and any specific requirements to help us route your request.

If you are interested in providing ChatGPT for your organization, you can reach out to our Sales team through our [sales contact form](https://openai.com/contact-sales/).  
  
Please note:

* Our Sales team may not be able to respond to every inquiry.
* For smaller teams and organizations, you may also be interested in our [ChatGPT Business](/en/articles/8792828-what-is-chatgpt-team) offering, available via self-service.
* **Billing & payment methods:** Self-serve ChatGPT Business and ChatGPT Pro subscriptions can only be purchased with a credit or debit card—we do not support invoices, purchase orders, or bank transfers. Organizations that require invoicing, POs, or wire transfers should instead submit the [sales contact form](https://openai.com/contact-sales/) to discuss a contracted plan. Please include your company name, number of seats, expected use case, and billing requirements to help us route your request.

## API Enterprise Contracts

If you are using the OpenAI API and have a monthly spend of approximately $10,000 or more, you can also contact our Sales team through the [sales contact form](https://openai.com/contact-sales/).  
  
For customers with lower usage needs:

* You can start immediately with a [pay-as-you-go API plan](https://openai.com/api/pricing/).
* We offer extensive [developer documentation](https://platform.openai.com/docs/), [API cookbooks](https://cookbook.openai.com/), and [production best practices](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/production-best-practices).
* For implementation support, explore the [OpenAI Community Forum](https://community.openai.com/) or our [Help Center](https://help.openai.com)

For compliance and security documentation, visit [trust.openai.com](https://trust.openai.com).

## Researcher Access Program

If you are a researcher studying the responsible deployment of AI, OpenAI offers opportunities for subsidized access through our [Researcher Access Program](https://openai.com/form/researcher-access-program/).
