# How can I find my past ChatGPT invoices?

Updated: 10 days ago

As a self-serve ChatGPT customer, you can retrieve your past invoices directly from the embedded billing portal. Depending on whether you are a Plus / Pro or Business subscriber, this location may slightly vary within your account settings.  
  
For invoices related to Apple or Google subscriptions please review our article [here](/en/articles/9030143-how-do-i-obtain-my-chatgpt-subscription-invoice-if-i-subscribed-from-the-apple-app-store).

### Plus / Pro

Navigate to your [*Settings > Account*](https://chatgpt.com/#settings/Account) page and click on *Payment > Manage*:

![ChatGPT Account settings page showing ChatGPT Pro subscription with the Payment section Manage button circled](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-234e60bcba36aa7d5d901551/ec7766282fc0abd4504eb7bcbe00e5b8/Payment_details.png)

From your customer billing portal, you can download a copy of your past invoices under the `Invoice History` section:

![Invoice History page listing paid ChatGPT Pro and Plus subscription invoices](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-ada0a4f7a8e0ebf5302be60b/892bdb3c2d06dd6f7dbcd6b30a1ad430/Screenshot_2025-09-22_at_9_13_25_E2_80_AFAM.png)

**Troubleshooting:** If your `Invoice History` section is missing or empty, make sure you’re signed into the correct ChatGPT account or workspace. Receipts for subscriptions started through the Apple App Store or Google Play won’t appear here—check your app-store purchase history instead. If you still can’t locate an invoice after switching accounts or reviewing app-store receipts, confirm you’re using the correct account or workspace and contact OpenAI Support with your account email.

### Business

From your [workspace settings](https://chatgpt.com/admin/billing), open the *Billing* tab, then select **Invoices** to view invoices available for that workspace/account. If none appear, confirm you are an owner on the correct workspace/account and that invoice records exist. If you still can’t locate invoices, contact OpenAI Support with your account email.

![Billing page with the Invoices tab selected and a past invoice dated November 22, 2024](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-868acc9d8c7f330ca43e046d/32936ea39114135c946ea8e8e7db1271/Screenshot_2025-09-22_at_10_07_59_E2_80_AFAM.png)

Similarly, if you click on Manage plan > Payment method, you should also be brought to your customer billing portal (which has your past invoices).

![ChatGPT Business plan billing page with Manage plan menu opened to Payment method](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-b08ce903548445364c15a116/5ea1bf59188448c190b113607ec5c550/Screenshot_2025-09-22_at_10_08_32_E2_80_AFAM.png)
