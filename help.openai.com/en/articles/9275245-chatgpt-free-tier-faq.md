# ChatGPT Free Tier FAQ

Updated: 10 days ago

ChatGPT is free to use, and Free tier users now have access to a large range of capabilities with GPT-5.5, including access to a series of tools and access to GPTs in our GPT store.  
  
In addition to advanced intelligence capabilities, Free tier users can now:

* Search the web and obtain up-to-date information
* Analyze and extract insights from your data
* Upload images or files in your prompts
* Discover and use GPTs
* Create images in ChatGPT
* Use Library with 500 MB of storage

Users on ChatGPT Plus have access to higher rate limits. [Learn more about ChatGPT Plus.](/en/articles/6950777-what-is-chatgpt-plus)  
  
We will start to show ads in the Free Tier in certain countries, so more people can benefit from our tools with fewer usage limits or without having to pay. Learn more about our approach to ads and your choices here.

## Can I create a GPT as a Free tier user?

Currently, all Paid plans have access to the GPT creator.

## How do rate limits work on the Free tier?

Free tier users can use GPT-5.5 only a limited number of times within a five hour window. We’ll notify you once you’ve reached the limit and invite you to upgrade to ChatGPT Plus.

![ChatGPT notice that the free image creation limit has been reached, with a Get Plus upgrade button](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-27194329df268eb3b5afc52d/d5aa7564a0ace094203811e04dd3644e/AD_4nXdlrqK2Mpjus7E3dZdk-WJCQC7qA9HPqMgAsJb7J1WONAUwJwQSpMjZ92UUOrmpk4lrp0k3ERw_JudbKZQCC4NCl5gEs-gukJERcVnGncR29v5bX3Pgun1fYHFz)

You can use GPTs as long as you can use GPT-5.5. When you hit your limit for GPT-5.5, you won't be able to use GPTs until your rate limit resets. Once you reach your GPT limit, we will output a message that states the time tomorrow that you can continue using GPTs.

![ChatGPT notice that the free-tier daily limit for GPTs has been reached, with a Get Plus button](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-cf5e7f266abb3c3535d27f3b/15de219f72e033fa5cb67d76fc356acf/AD_4nXfTb0M5B_OoSZvpuYCf0vWfkPuTBWlHBqx7MbP7ZtKvYER6co18Ylt-4kmDPppXYOiW-TTOqqi2iKpjljsQGcg3CZZ9SDaxMDpRyx2eyW6Ir2fWogUaPmUqVDGi)

The following tools have usage rate limits that are separate from the GPT-5.5 text rate limit:

* Data analysis with ChatGPT
* File and image uploads
* Create images in ChatGPT

If you reach a rate limit for any of these tools, you will need to wait for a later time to use any of these tools.

## Will my rate limit reset when I upgrade my account to ChatGPT Plus?

Yes. If you reach a rate limit on your Free tier account, your usage rates will reset when you update your account to a Plus plan, a Pro plan, or a Business plan.

## Is performance for GPTs different for users on the Free tier?

GPT usage on the Free tier is subject to the same limitations as ChatGPT. GPT advanced functionality, which includes data analysis, file uploads, and image creation, is subject to stricter rate limits on the Free tier than on paid tiers. As a Free user, your GPT-5.5 text rate limit will also apply to GPTs.
