# How can I change the billing details on my invoice?

Updated: 3 days ago

## ChatGPT

To update your billing details for future invoices:

1. Click on your profile icon in the bottom left corner on [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) and go to Settings.
2. Navigate to the **Account tab**.
3. Click on Payment, then select **Manage**.
4. Under **Billing information**, select **Update information** to change your details.

**Note:** These changes will only apply to future invoices. If you need a past invoice reissued with updated details, please contact us via the chat widget in the bottom-right corner of the Help Center.

## API

To update the details on your API invoices:

1. Go to your [billing preferences](https://platform.openai.com/settings/organization/billing/preferences) within your API settings.
2. Update your billing information as needed.

**Note:** Just like with ChatGPT, these changes will only affect future invoices. For a reissue of past invoices, please reach out via the chat widget in the bottom-right corner of the Help Center.
