# Codex Referral Promotions

Terms for Codex referral offers

Updated: 2 days ago

# Promotion Overview

Eligible users may see an in-product Codex referral offer. Depending on the offer, eligible users may be able to invite another person to try Codex and receive a promotional benefit after the invited person completes the required action.  
  
Referral benefits may include Codex usage credits, a Codex rate-limit reset, temporary additional Codex usage, or another benefit described in the offer. The exact benefit, cap, expiration, and redemption requirements may vary by plan, workspace, region, and promotion.  
  
Rewards are not granted when an invite is sent. They are granted only after the invited person accepts the invite, meets eligibility requirements, completes the qualifying Codex action, and passes any required checks. Some offers may require the invited person to join a specific ChatGPT Business workspace. OpenAI may modify or wind down referral promotions with at least 30 days' notice.

# Eligibility Requirements

Eligibility depends on the offer shown in-product. Receiving an invite or seeing an offer does not guarantee redemption.  
  
  
**For workspace referrals, workspace eligibility may require:**

* The workspace must be on an eligible ChatGPT Business configuration described below.
* The workspace must be in good standing, including payments.
* The workspace is not on a free trial.
* Referral promotions have not been disabled by a workspace admin.

**Referrer eligibility may require:**

* An eligible ChatGPT plan or workspace as described in the offer.
* Must have a ChatGPT seat subscription.
* An account, subscription, or workspace in good standing.
* Prior Codex usage or an eligible Codex usage, credit, or rate-limit event.
* A professional or matching workspace email domain, when required by the offer.
* Remaining within any referral caps, cooldowns, or redemption limits described in the offer.

**Invitee eligibility may require:**

* Accepting the referral through the official referral flow described in the offer.
* Using an eligible email address or account as described in the offer.
* Joining the relevant workspace, if the offer is tied to a ChatGPT Business workspace.
* Being new to Codex or having no recent Codex usage during the lookback period specified in the offer.
* Completing the qualifying Codex action described in the offer.

Duplicate redemptions, self-invites, address aliases used to evade eligibility, device-linked self-referrals, and other suspected fraudulent or abusive activity are not eligible.

# FAQs

## How do Codex referral promotions work?

If you are eligible, you may see an in-product offer to invite another person to try Codex. If the invited person accepts the invite, meets the requirements, and completes the qualifying Codex action, eligible participants may receive the benefit described in the offer.

## What benefits can I receive?

Benefits vary by offer. They may include Codex usage credits, a Codex rate-limit reset, temporary additional Codex usage, or another promotional benefit described in the offer.

## What benefits can the invited person receive?

The invited person may receive the benefit described in the offer after accepting the invite and completing the required action, if they meet eligibility requirements.

## How do I activate a referral reward?

The invited person must accept the referral invite and complete the qualifying Codex action. If the offer is tied to a ChatGPT Business workspace, the invited person may also need to join that workspace. Rewards are applied after those steps and any eligibility checks are complete.

## How many rewards can I earn?

Referral caps, cooldowns, and redemption limits vary by offer and are shown in the applicable offer. Some offers may limit the number of rewards per person, workspace, subscription, or time period.

## Can existing ChatGPT Business workspaces participate in workspace-specific referrals?

Yes, if the workspace and users meet the offer's eligibility requirements, the workspace is in good standing, and referral promotions have not been disabled by a workspace admin.

## Who can be invited?

Invitee eligibility depends on the offer. Requirements may include being new to Codex, having no recent Codex usage during a specified lookback period, using an eligible email address, joining a relevant workspace, and completing a qualifying Codex action.

## What if the invited person already uses ChatGPT or Codex?

Eligibility depends on the offer. Some offers may allow existing ChatGPT users if they are new to Codex or are not yet part of the relevant workspace. Other offers may exclude people with recent Codex usage.

## What if the same person receives multiple referral invites?

Only the first qualifying referral invite can count toward reward redemption. Duplicate invites do not create additional rewards.

## What happens if I am no longer rate-limited by the time the invited person completes the required action?

The reward may still be granted if the referral remains within any applicable redemption period and all other eligibility requirements are met.

## Are referral credits API credits?

No, unless the offer explicitly says otherwise. Credits issued under these promotions are generally for the Codex or ChatGPT experience described in the offer.

## Are rate-limit resets credits?

No. Rate-limit resets and temporary usage windows are not credits and do not create a cash, API, or transferable credit balance.

## Can workspace admins turn referral promotions off?

ChatGPT Business workspace admins may disable referral promotions for their workspace. If disabled, eligible users should not see workspace referral offers and new workspace referral rewards will not be granted.

## Do referral rewards expire?

Referral rewards may expire as described in the referral offer or applicable terms. For offers that grant a banked Codex rate-limit reset, the reset must be used within 30 days after it is added to your bank, unless the offer says otherwise. Check the offer for expiration and redemption details.

## Can OpenAI end or change these promotions?

OpenAI may modify or wind down referral promotions with at least 30 days' notice. OpenAI may also revoke or cancel rewards sooner in cases of suspected misuse, fraud, or other violations, in compliance with law, or as permitted under applicable terms.

## Can I transfer or sell a referral offer?

No. Referral offers are intended only for eligible users and eligible invitees. They may not be transferred, sold, or exchanged.
