# How can I delete my OpenAI account in the ChatGPT Android app?

How to delete your OpenAI account from the ChatGPT Android app

Updated: 3 days ago

**Deleting your account is permanent and can not be undone.**

**Deleting your account will prevent you from using the account to access OpenAI services, including ChatGPT and API.** You will NOT be able to create a new account using the same email address. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except we may retain a limited set of data for longer where required or permitted by law.

If you subscribed through the **Google Play Store**, deleting your OpenAI account **does not** cancel that mobile subscription. You must cancel it in the **Google Play Store** to stop future charges. Please refer to the following article for more information:

[How to cancel a subscription in the ChatGPT Android app](/en/articles/8258076-how-to-cancel-a-subscription-in-the-chatgpt-android-app)

**Note**: Data retention for certain services may be affected by recent legal developments – please see our [blog post](https://openai.com/index/response-to-nyt-data-demands/) for more details.  
  
If you wish to delete your account, tap the two lines located in the top left corner of the screen to open the menu.  
  
At the bottom right corner of the menu, click the dots to the right of your name to open **Settings**.  
  
Navigate to **Data Controls** and click **Delete Account**.  
  
Then in the confirmation modal that appears click Delete Account to delete your ChatGPT account.
