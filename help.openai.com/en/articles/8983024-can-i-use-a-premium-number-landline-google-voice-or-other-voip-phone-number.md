# Can I use a premium number, landline, Google Voice, or other VoIP phone number?

Updated: 10 days ago

We do **not** support use of landlines, VoIP, Google Voice, or premium numbers at this time. All of those types of phone numbers are often associated with higher instances of fraud or abuse.  
  
For this reason we only support completing phone verification via mobile phone numbers over an SMS text message. In [selected regions](/en/articles/8983038-which-countries-do-you-support-for-whatsapp-phone-verification), we also support Whatsapp verification.
