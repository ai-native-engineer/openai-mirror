# Something went wrong. Please make sure your device's date and time are set properly. Check that your internet connection is stable, then restart the app and try again. (DeviceCheckError)

Updated: 10 days ago

This error typically indicates that we were unable to generate a token for your specific device. This guide outlines troubleshooting steps to help resolve this error:

1. **Verify Date and Time Settings**
2. **Check Your Internet Connection**
3. **Update Your App and Operating System**
4. **Device Integrity Checks**

   1. Modifications like rooting or jailbreaking may cause this error.
   2. Work with Apple or Google to ensure your device passes integrity checks:

      1. iOS: [DeviceCheck](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/devicecheck)
      2. *Note: Integrity check results can be cached for up to 24 hours. Clear your app data before retrying.*

## Additional Troubleshooting

1. Log in via your browser.
2. Try a different device.
3. Check our [Status Page](https://status.openai.com/) for any known outages or issues

If none of the above steps resolve the issue, please capture a screenshot of the error and reach out to our support team for further assistance.
