# How do I log out of all of my devices?

Discover how to disable all of your active ChatGPT and Platform sessions across all devices.

Updated: 10 days ago

You can find the option to log out of all active sessions across all devices in the Settings page located in [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/?oai-dm=1#settings/Security) or the [OpenAI developer platform](https://platform.openai.com/settings/profile?tab=security) security settings.

# ChatGPT

To log out of all active sessions on ChatGPT across all devices:

1. Select your profile icon on the top-right corner of the page.
2. Click on [Settings](https://chatgpt.com/?oai-dm=1#settings/Security) in the pop-up menu.
3. In the Settings modal, select Security and click on **Log out all**. You will be immediately logged out of the existing session.

![ChatGPT Settings Security page with the Log out all button for signing out of all devices](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-6d06752c4544668f5da25786/a10a3b067b2959d36c491efec5c9f14f/image.png)

Please note that it may take up to 30 minutes for other active ChatGPT sessions to be logged out.

# API Platform

To log out of all active sessions on the API Platform across all devices:

1. Select gear icon on the top-right corner of the page.
2. Click on **Your Profile** under the options available at the left of the page.
3. Under the Security tab, click on **Log out all**. You will be immediately logged out of the existing session.

Please note that all active API Platform sessions across all devices will be immediately closed.
