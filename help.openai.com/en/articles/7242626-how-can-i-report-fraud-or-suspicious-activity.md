# How can I report fraud or suspicious activity?

Updated: 3 days ago

At OpenAI, your account security is a top priority. If you notice suspicious or unauthorized activity on your account, please follow the steps below to protect your information and report the issue to us immediately.

## Steps to Secure Your Account:

1. **Update Your Password:**

   * Navigate to your [OpenAI account settings](https://platform.openai.com/account).
   * Choose a strong, unique password that you haven't used elsewhere.
2. **Log Out of All Devices:**

   * In your account settings, select "Log out of all sessions" to immediately disconnect your account from any unauthorized devices.
3. **Rotate Your API Key (For API Users Only):**

   * If you're using OpenAI API services and suspect your API key has been compromised, rotate your API key immediately by navigating to your [API keys settings](https://platform.openai.com/api-keys).

## How to Report Fraud or Suspicious Activity:

If you encounter suspicious activity, please send an email to support@openai.com with the following details:

* **Your registered email address**
* **Detailed description of the suspicious activity**
* **Amount overcharged, if applicable**
* **Relevant screenshots or additional details**

Our support team will review your report and respond promptly to assist you.
