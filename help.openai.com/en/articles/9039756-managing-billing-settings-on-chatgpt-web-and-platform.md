# Managing Billing Settings on ChatGPT Web and Platform

Updated: 3 days ago

If you need to manage billing for ChatGPT or the API platform, first make sure you are in the right product. ChatGPT and the API platform use separate billing systems, so charges and billing history are managed separately.

## How to manage billing for ChatGPT web subscriptions (Free/Go/Plus/Pro)

If you subscribed to ChatGPT on the web, open [ChatGPT billing settings](https://chatgpt.com/#settings/Billing) or follow these steps:

1. Log in to ChatGPT on the web
2. Open **Settings**
3. Select **Billing**

Use the options on the **Billing** pane to manage your subscription and payment methods, and to view available billing history for your account.

## How to manage billing for ChatGPT Business or Enterprise

If you are an owner of a ChatGPT Business or Enterprise workspace, open [workspace billing settings](https://chatgpt.com/admin/billing) to view and manage billing details for your workspace.

## How to manage billing for the API platform

If you are an owner of an API organization, open the [API billing overview](https://platform.openai.com/account/billing/overview) to view billing details for the API platform.

## If you use ChatGPT on more than one platform

ChatGPT subscriptions can be billed through the web, Apple App Store, or Google Play Store. If you have subscriptions on more than one platform, you may be charged separately. To check which platform manages your subscription and avoid duplicate charges, see [How do I avoid being charged twice if I subscribe to ChatGPT on iOS, Android, and the web?](/en/articles/20001043-how-do-i-avoid-being-charged-twice-if-i-subscribe-to-chatgpt-on-ios-android-and-the-web).
