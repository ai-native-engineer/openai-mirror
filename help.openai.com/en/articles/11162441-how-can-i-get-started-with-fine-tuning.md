# How can I get started with fine-tuning?

Fine-tune models for better results and efficiency

Updated: 10 days ago

If you're interested in fine tuning an OpenAI model to better adapt it to a specific task, refer to our [fine-tuning guide](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/fine-tuning#use-a-fine-tuned-model). This guide is suitable for developers working with our API. If you're interested in getting better responses from ChatGPT, review our [prompt engineering guide](/en/articles/10032626-prompt-engineering-best-practices-for-chatgpt) for ChatGPT.

## Which models are available for fine-tuning?

Check the [model optimization and fine-tuning guide](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/model-optimization) and your organization's `/v1/fine_tuning/model_limits` response for current fine-tuning model availability.
