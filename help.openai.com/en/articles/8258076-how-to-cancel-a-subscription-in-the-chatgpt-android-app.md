# How to cancel a subscription in the ChatGPT Android app

Details for Android about cancelling your ChatGPT subscription in the Google Play Store.

Updated: 3 days ago

**Important points**

1. Uninstalling the ChatGPT Android app will not cancel your ChatGPT subscription.
2. Subscriptions originally created on [chatgpt.com](http://chatgpt.com/) must be cancelled on [chatgpt.com](http://chatgpt.com/).
3. Subscriptions created via Google Subscriptions can be cancelled from the Google Play Store app or [Google Play website](https://play.google.com) while signed in with the same Google account you used to subscribe to ChatGPT.

# Cancel via Google Play Store app

1. On your Android device, open the Google Play Store.
2. Check to make sure you’re signed in to the correct Google account you used to sign up for your ChatGPT subscription.
3. Tap Menu > **Subscriptions**.
4. Select the ChatGPT subscription.
5. Tap **Cancel subscription**.
6. Follow the instructions provided.

# Cancel via Google Play website

***Note:*** *Uninstalling the ChatGPT Android app will not cancel your subscription.*

1. On your computer, go to [subscriptions in Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions).
2. Click **Manage** on the ChatGPT subscription.
3. Click **Cancel subscription**.
4. Within the confirmation pop-up, select a reason.
5. Click **Continue**.

# Subscription Billing and Cancellation Information

* Your Google Play Store account will be charged 24 hours prior to the end of the current subscription period.
* Subscription features will remain in your account until the end of the current paid period after canceling.
* For detailed instructions on how to cancel a subscription for Android, check out Google's official guide to [Cancel, pause, or change a subscription on Google Play](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/7018481?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid&oco=1#zippy=%2Ccancel-a-subscription-on-the-google-play-app)
* Deleting your OpenAI account will automatically cancel your ChatGPT subscription to prevent unwanted charges.
* For additional cancellation options, including on other platforms, see [How to Cancel My ChatGPT Subscription](/en/articles/8157051-how-to-cancel-my-chatgpt-subscription).
