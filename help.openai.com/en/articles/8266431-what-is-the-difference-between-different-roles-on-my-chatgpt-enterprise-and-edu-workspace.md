# What is the difference between different roles on my ChatGPT Enterprise and Edu workspace?

Exploring workspace roles in ChatGPT Enterprise and Edu

Updated: 14 days ago

# Role types

Currently available user roles are:

* Member
* Analytics viewer
* Admin
* Owner

Here are the permissions assigned to each role:

## Summary

* **Members** can fully use ChatGPT and create GPTs but have no admin privileges.
* **Analytics viewers** are members who can also view workspace analytics.
* **Admins** help manage users and groups and perform routine administrative tasks.
* **Owners** have full access, including billing, identity management, and workspace configuration.
