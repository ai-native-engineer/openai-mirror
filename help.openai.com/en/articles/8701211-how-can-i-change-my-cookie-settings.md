# How can I change my cookie settings?

Changing your cookie settings on ChatGPT, OpenAI and API Platform.

Updated: 2 days ago

Depending on your region, you may be able to choose which cookies are set.

### For Help Center (help.openai.com)

1. Go to help.openai.com
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
3. Click **Cookie Preferences** in the footer

### For ChatGPT (chatgpt.com)

1. Go to chatgpt.com
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
3. Click **Cookie Preferences** in the footer

### For OpenAI (openai.com)

1. Go to chatgpt.com
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
3. Click **Manage Cookies** in the footer

### For Platform (platform.openai.com)

You can manage it both while logged in and when logged out.  
  
While logged in:

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to the top right corner and click on your Profile icon
3. Click **Manage cookies**

While logged out:

1. Navigate to top right corner to the menu (2 horizontal lines)
2. Click **Manage cookies**

"Manage cookies" screen will navigate you through the available options.
