# Why am I getting an error message stating that I've reached my usage limit?

What to do when you get an error message about API usage limits.

Updated: 10 days ago

To resolve this error, you need to [increase your monthly budget](https://platform.openai.com/account/limits). To set your limit over the approved usage limit (normally, $120.00/month), please review your [Usage Limits](https://platform.openai.com/account/limits) for information regarding advancing to the next tier.  
  
If your needs exceed what's available in the 'Increasing your limits' tier or you have a unique use case, [contact our support team](https://intercom.help/openai/en/articles/6614161-how-can-i-contact-support) request for a higher limit. Our team will look into your request and respond as soon as we can.
