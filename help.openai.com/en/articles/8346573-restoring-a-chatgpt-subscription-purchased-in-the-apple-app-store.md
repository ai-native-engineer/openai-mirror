# Restoring a ChatGPT subscription purchased in the Apple App Store

How to restore your purchase of a ChatGPT subscription made in the Apple App Store in the ChatGPT iOS app.

Updated: 14 days ago

Users that subscribed to ChatGPT **in the** **Apple App Store** can use the `Restore purchases` feature in the ChatGPT iOS app to restore their subscription on their iOS device.

Under **Settings** -> **Account** -> **Restore purchases**

![ChatGPT mobile Settings menu highlighting the Restore purchases option under Account](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-1b0873e0f8809f085d4ed2b7/60742ad05f4760948806e0bd845692c4/image.png)

Note: Users who subscribed to **on the web** will need to manage their subscription at [chatgpt.com](http://chatgpt.com/).
