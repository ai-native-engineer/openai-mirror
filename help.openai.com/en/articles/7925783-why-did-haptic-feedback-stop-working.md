# Why did Haptic Feedback stop working?

Updated: 14 days ago

The app will automatically disable the Haptic Feedback (vibration) feature when one of the following conditions is met:

1. When going on low power mode Haptic Feedback will be disabled
2. When the iPhone battery is below 20% the Haptic Feedback will be disabled
