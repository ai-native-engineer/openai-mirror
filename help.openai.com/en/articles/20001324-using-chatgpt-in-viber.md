# Using ChatGPT in Viber

Learn how ChatGPT-powered features work in Rakuten Viber, including account connection, usage limits, data use, and where to get support.

Updated: 10 hours ago

# Overview

ChatGPT-powered features are available in Rakuten Viber through an integration between Viber and OpenAI. These features let you use ChatGPT in parts of Viber, including message translation, AI summaries, AI Polish, Image Remix, the ChatGPT tab, and @ChatGPT mentions in chats where available.  
  
Availability may vary by region, Viber app version, account, and chat type. For current Viber-specific steps and availability, see [Viber's AI on Viber article](https://help.viber.com/hc/en-us/articles/31738053588509-AI-on-Viber).

# What you can do with ChatGPT in Viber

Depending on availability in Viber, you can use ChatGPT-powered features to:

* Translate a message.
* Summarize a shared link or unread group chat messages.
* Polish a draft message before sending it.
* Remix an image using a prompt or preset style.
* Use the ChatGPT tab for a direct ChatGPT conversation in Viber.
* Mention @ChatGPT in supported private or group chats so ChatGPT can respond in that chat.

ChatGPT can make mistakes. Check important information before relying on it. [Learn more about ChatGPT.](/en/articles/12677804-what-is-chatgpt-faq)

# Using your ChatGPT account in Viber

You can use some ChatGPT-powered features without connecting a ChatGPT account. If you reach Viber's daily limit or want higher limits where available, Viber may ask you to connect or create a ChatGPT account.  
  
When you connect your ChatGPT account, Viber opens a secure OpenAI sign-in or sign-up flow. Your use of ChatGPT-powered features may then be associated with your ChatGPT account and handled under OpenAI's standard retention policies.  
  
You can disconnect your ChatGPT account from Viber settings. After disconnecting, your daily limits may be reduced and some ChatGPT-powered features may not be available.

# Data and privacy

When you use a ChatGPT-powered feature in Viber, Viber sends OpenAI the information needed to provide that feature. Depending on the feature, this may include the message, draft, image, prompt, link content, messages with an @ChatGPT mention, a Viber-generated hashed user ID, timestamps, and approximate location such as country or city.  
  
Treat information shared through these features like information you choose to provide directly to ChatGPT, especially if it includes sensitive or personal information.  
  
If you connect your ChatGPT account, your activity with Viber's AI features is associated with that account. Conversations in Viber’s “ChatGPT” tab are stored in your ChatGPT account’s conversation history, may be used to inform memories (if memories is enabled), and may be used to improve OpenAI’s models if training is enabled in your settings.  
  
Outside of ChatGPT tab conversations, OpenAI does not use data sent from Viber's ChatGPT-powered features in Viber personal and group chats to train its models.  
  
To learn more about managing your ChatGPT data settings, see [Data Controls FAQ](https://help.openai.com/articles/7730893).  
  
Data handling, retention, and permissions in Viber follow Viber’s policies for its services.

# Limits and availability

Viber may limit how often you can use ChatGPT-powered features each day. Daily limits may differ depending on whether you connect a ChatGPT account and which ChatGPT plan you have.  
  
If a feature does not appear, it may not be available in your region, Viber version, account, or chat type. Update Viber and review [Viber's AI on Viber article](https://help.viber.com/hc/en-us/articles/31738053588509-AI-on-Viber) for current availability.

# Getting help

For Viber app issues, chat behavior, feature availability, Viber limits, or Viber-owned account settings, contact [Viber Support](https://help.viber.com/hc/en-us).  
  
Contact OpenAI Support only for issues with your OpenAI account, such as signing in to your ChatGPT account, connecting your ChatGPT account to Viber, or disconnecting it.

# FAQ

## Can I use ChatGPT in Viber without a ChatGPT account?

Yes. Some ChatGPT-powered features may be available without connecting a ChatGPT account, subject to Viber availability and daily limits. Connecting a ChatGPT account will increase your available daily usage where supported.

## Is ChatGPT in Viber the same as using ChatGPT directly?

No. Viber provides ChatGPT-powered features inside the Viber app. The exact features, limits, and UI are controlled by Viber.

## Can other people see when I use ChatGPT in Viber?

It depends on the feature. For example, @ChatGPT replies in a group chat are visible in that group chat, while some features such as AI Polish may be visible only to you before you share or send content. Review Viber's feature-specific guidance before sharing generated content.

## Does OpenAI train its models on Viber chat data?

If you have connected your ChatGPT account, conversations in Viber’s “ChatGPT” tab follow the controls in your ChatGPT account, and may be used to improve OpenAI’s models if training is enabled in your settings. [Learn more](/en/articles/7730893-data-controls-faq) about your data controls in ChatGPT.  
  
Outside of ChatGPT tab conversations, OpenAI does not use data sent from Viber's ChatGPT-powered features in Viber personal and group chats to train its models.

## How do I disconnect my ChatGPT account from Viber?

You can disconnect your ChatGPT account in Viber settings. For current steps, see [Viber's AI on Viber article](https://help.viber.com/hc/en-us/articles/31738053588509-AI-on-Viber). After disconnecting, daily limits may be reduced and some features may no longer be available.
