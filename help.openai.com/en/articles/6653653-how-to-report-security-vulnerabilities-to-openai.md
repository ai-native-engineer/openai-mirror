# How to Report Security Vulnerabilities to OpenAI

Our policy and guidelines for testing and disclosure

Updated: 3 days ago

If you are interested in finding and reporting security vulnerabilities in OpenAI's services, please read and follow our [Coordinated Vulnerability Disclosure Policy](https://openai.com/security/disclosure/).  
  
This policy explains how to:

* Request authorization for testing
* Identify what types of testing are in-scope and out-of-scope
* Communicate with us securely

We appreciate your efforts to help us improve our security and protect our users and technology.
