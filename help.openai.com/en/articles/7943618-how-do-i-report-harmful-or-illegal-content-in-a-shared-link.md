# How do I report harmful or illegal content in a shared link?

Reporting a problematic shared link from ChatGPT

Updated: 3 days ago

Shared Links is GA on Web and iOS.

Android is coming soon!

You can report a shared conversation to us by clicking “Report Content”. You will be asked to describe the issue with the content and to identify the nature of your report. You do not need to be signed in to submit content reports.
