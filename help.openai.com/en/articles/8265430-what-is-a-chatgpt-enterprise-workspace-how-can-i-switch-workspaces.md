# What is a ChatGPT Enterprise workspace? How can I switch workspaces?

Learn how ChatGPT Enterprise workspaces are structured, how they differ from personal accounts, and how to switch between them.

Updated: 3 days ago

## What is a ChatGPT Enterprise workspace?

A **ChatGPT Enterprise workspace** is a dedicated environment for your organization within ChatGPT. It provides:

* Centralized management of members, billing, and security settings
* Access to business-grade features and controls
* Separate conversations, custom GPTs, and files from any personal use
* Enterprise-grade data handling and privacy, including default opt-out from model training

When you join a company's ChatGPT Enterprise workspace:

* Your conversations and files within that workspace are owned and managed by the organization.
* Your workspace activity is governed by the organization's policies and administrative controls.

**Note:** Some organizations may require you to **merge your personal workspace into the Enterprise workspace** when you accept an invite.

If you merge, all your chats, files, and custom GPTs from your personal workspace will move into the organization's control.

**Merging is permanent** and cannot be undone.

You may also have situations where you retain both:

* A **personal workspace** (your individual account)
* A **separate Enterprise workspace** managed by your organization

In that case, they remain fully distinct.  
  
---

## How do I switch between workspaces?

If you have access to multiple workspaces, you can switch at any time:

1. Click your **profile icon** in the top-right corner of the ChatGPT app.
2. A list of your available workspaces will appear.
3. Click on the **workspace name** you want to access.

You will immediately enter the selected workspace’s environment, with its own conversations, settings, and resources.

## Key Points to Remember

* **Workspaces are fully isolated**: Data and resources do not overlap between personal and Enterprise environments.
* **Enterprise workspaces** are controlled by your organization's admins.
* **Some enterprises may require merging** your personal workspace when you join — merging is irreversible.
* **Switching workspaces** simply changes which environment you are working in; it does not move or copy data across workspaces.
