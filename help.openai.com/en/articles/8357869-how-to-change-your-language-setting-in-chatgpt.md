# How to change your language setting in ChatGPT

Set your browser language to one of our supported languages

Updated: 10 days ago

When you access ChatGPT on a browser or mobile device, we automatically detect your browser or mobile device’s language and update ChatGPT’s language to match for [supported languages](#h_513834920e).  
  
In our recommended browser, Google Chrome, you can find your browser’s preferred language by going to chrome://settings/languages. If you want to use ChatGPT in a different language, you can update your browser’s preferred language or [update your settings](#h_73fa3a58dd) in ChatGPT.

# How do I use ChatGPT in a different language?

Please note that you can only update the ChatGPT language setting if you are logged in.

## On the browser

Click on **your profile icon > Settings > General > Language >** Select your preferred language in the drop-down.

![ChatGPT Settings > General with the Language menu open and Auto-detect selected](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-eed40183c928bf51bd64084b/ff4dc90a645c4ceadd871227977997c8/image.png)

## On the mobile app

1. Open the sidebar and select your profile icon at the bottom of the screen.
2. Under App, select the Language option.

![ChatGPT mobile Settings page with Language set to English](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-e91c2d133e8606e189493d17/9118175158e4100ec5e81660df7dfcf2/iKcRgEe4Rt669lGxBN1NtWe17NWB-0TdL6YR5Z6-pvJQ8UplCuR_sfQLK0F51vUWcQIV2S1p3-0lr_3_z-hNLLOZ3CEyqWhxHM_41lzJcjp2fRBh4ZYbJT79WxMTHvaS)

1. Select your preferred language in the drop-down.
