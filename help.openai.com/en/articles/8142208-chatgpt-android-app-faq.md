## Where can I download and install the ChatGPT Android app from the Google Play Store?

You can find and install the official ChatGPT Android app in two ways:

* **Search**: Open the Google Play Store and search for **"openai chatgpt"** to ensure you're downloading the official app published by OpenAI.
* **Direct Link**: [Download the ChatGPT Android app here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.openai.chatgpt).

⚠️ **Important**: Make sure you are downloading the app **published by OpenAI** to ensure you have the official and secure version.

## How do I check the ChatGPT Android app version?

1. Open the sidebar and tap your **profile icon**
2. Select **About**
3. The version will be displayed under **ChatGPT for Android**

## How to delete my account in the ChatGPT Android app

**Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.**

**Deleting your account will prevent you from using the account to access OpenAI services, including ChatGPT and API.** You will NOT be able to create a new account using the same email address. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except we may retain a limited set of data for longer where required or permitted by law.

See [**How to delete your account**](/en/articles/6378407-how-can-i-delete-my-account)**.**

To delete your ChatGPT account you can navigate to **Data Controls** and click **Delete Account**.

![Image](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-1976d7f0783a567bc8fb2522/15bc84dd5128367ef129a71611f4049e/android-delete-export.jpg)

Then in the confirmation modal that appears click Delete Account to delete your ChatGPT account.

## How to export all your chat data

If you wish to export your chat conversation data:

1. Open the sidebar and tap your **profile icon**
2. Select **Data Controls**
3. Select **Export Data**

Please note that the downloadable zip file will be sent to your registered email address and the link will expire after 24 hours. The export process may take some time, depending on the amount of data that is being requested.

## Do you collect my precise location data through the ChatGPT Android App?

Yes, if you choose to turn on precise location sharing, which you can learn more about in this [help article](/en/articles/9237897-chatgpt-search#location). Otherwise, by default our Android App doesn’t access your device’s Location, Bluetooth, or other services to collect or approximate your precise location data. We generally do collect your IP address, however. IP addresses can be used to estimate the device’s coarse location, for e.g., country, state, and city information.  
  
---

## Do you store audio clips from the speech-to-text feature?

**No**.  
  
We send audio clips from our speech-to-text feature to our servers so that we can use our Whisper API to transcribe them, but we won’t retain voice clips beyond what’s necessary to complete the transcription.  
  
Audio clips are also briefly copied to your Android device’s temporary storage/cache, and are deleted in accordance with your operating system’s design. After transcription, we do not access or use audio clips that are in your Android device’s temp storage/cache for any purpose.  
  
---

## Do you train your models on audio clips from the speech-to-text feature?

No. We only use the audio clips to prepare a transcription using our Whisper API. The clips are then deleted, which means that we will not use those audio clips to improve our models. The transcriptions get used as inputs to ChatGPT and appear in conversation histories, which may be used to improve our models (depending on user settings).

## When I try to login to the ChatGPT Android app I'm getting a "Something went wrong" error. What does this mean and how do I login?

This can happen when your Google Play Store version is out of date. Please update your Google Play Store version then wait a little while and try again.  
  
  
**How to update the Google Play Store version**

1. Open the Google Play Store app.
2. At the top right, tap the profile icon.
3. Tap **Settings** > **About** > **Play Store version**.
4. You'll get a message that tells you if the Play Store is up to date. Tap **Got it**.

   1. If an update is available, it will automatically download and install in a few minutes.

## Is Google Chrome the default browser?

The ChatGPT Android app attempts to login with Chrome or Brave if they are installed on the device, and shows an error if these browsers are not available. If this behavior changes in a future release we'll keep this knowledge updated.  
  
We recommend using Google Chrome as your default browser to resolve a number of common login issues with the ChatGPT Android app.
