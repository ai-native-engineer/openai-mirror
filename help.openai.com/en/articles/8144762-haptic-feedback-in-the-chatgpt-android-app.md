# Haptic feedback in the ChatGPT Android app

Understanding haptic feedback support in the ChatGPT Android app

Updated: 10 days ago

Haptic feedback can be toggled on and off in the Setting page of the app.

1. Open the sidebar
2. Tap your **Profile Icon**
3. Tap **Personalization**
4. Toggle on or off **Haptic Feedback**

The haptic feedback setting will only be visible for users who:

1. Have a device running Android 12+.
2. Your device manufacturer supports the specific haptic patterns OpenAI uses for the ChatGPT Android app.

The haptic settings will be hidden if both of the above requirements are not met.
