# What is ChatGPT: FAQ

Updated: 2 days ago

Welcome! This FAQ walks you through the basics: how to start, what you can do, and how to control your data and privacy.

## What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI assistant you can use for everyday tasks like brainstorming, writing, studying, planning, math, coding, analyzing images/files, and more. It’s trained to follow your instructions in a conversation. [Learn more about ChatGPT](/en/articles/6783457-what-is-chatgpt%23.webm?utm_source=chatgpt.com).

## Where can I use ChatGPT?

* **Web:** chatgpt.com (you can even try it without an account in supported regions).
* **Mobile apps:** iOS and Android.

## How do I start my first chat?

Open ChatGPT, type what you need in the message box, and send. Select **New chat** any time to start fresh.

## What can I try first?

* Ask follow-up questions (ChatGPT remembers context within a chat).
* Upload a file or image to analyze, summarize, or transform (available on paid plans with data analysis).
* Create an image (“Create a watercolor logo of a mountain at sunrise”). ChatGPT images include C2PA provenance metadata.

## Can ChatGPT search the web?

Yes. ChatGPT can search the web and cite sources when it needs current information. You can also click the web-search icon or use the “/Search” shortcut in supported experiences.

## What is Temporary Chat?

Temporary chats don’t show in history, don’t use/create memories, and aren’t used to train models. Start a temporary chat from the model menu when you want extra privacy for a specific conversation.

## How do I export my data or delete my account?

* **Export:** Go to **Settings → Data Controls → Export** to receive a download of your data.
* **Delete account:** You can delete your account in **Settings** or via the Privacy Portal. Deletion is permanent and processed within standard timelines.

## Which plans are available?

* **Free:** Core ChatGPT with basic limits.
* **Plus / Pro:** Access to our latest models and tools; higher limits; voice, images, data analysis, and more. Details and purchase flow live in **Settings → Subscription** and on plan pages.
* **Business & Enterprise:** Collaboration features, higher limits, admin controls, and enterprise privacy commitments. See **Business FAQ** and **Enterprise pages** for details.

## Are there message limits?

Yes, limits vary by plan and model and can change over time to keep performance stable (Plus has higher limits than Free; Business/Enterprise higher than Plus). Check model-specific entries and release notes for current allowances.

## Can I share a conversation?

Yes. Use **Share** to create a link to a read-only copy of a conversation you can send to others.

## Where can I read the privacy policy and usage rules?

See OpenAI’s [**Privacy Policy**](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy/?utm_source=chatgpt.com') and [**Usage Policies**](https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies/?utm_source=chatgpt.com) for full details on data, safety, and acceptable use.

## How do I contact support?

From help.openai.com, use the chat widget in the bottom-right corner to contact support or review guided articles.
