## What should I expect from OpenAI on social media?

* We use the official handles listed above to communicate news, announcements, and updates about OpenAI.
* We’ll **never** go into details of your account over social media. If a social media handle reaches out to you asking for your account details or offering to help with your account, please do not engage.
* Never share your account login details over social media.

## Authentic OpenAI numbers

1-800-ChatGPT is an experimental feature that enables another way to access ChatGPT.
