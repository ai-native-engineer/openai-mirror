# Setting up ChatGPT with Apple Intelligence

Connect ChatGPT to Apple Intelligence features, customize your experience, and manage your settings anytime.

Updated: 15 days ago

You can connect ChatGPT to Apple Intelligence features like Writing Tools and Siri by setting up the ChatGPT extension on your device.

## How to Set Up ChatGPT with Apple Intelligence

1. **Open Settings** on your device.
2. Tap **Apple Intelligence & Siri**.
3. Tap **ChatGPT**, then tap **Set Up**.
4. Choose one of the following options:

   * **Use ChatGPT without an account:** Tap **Enable ChatGPT**. *Note: If you later want to sign in with a ChatGPT account, go to Settings > Apple Intelligence & Siri > ChatGPT > Sign In.*
   * **Use ChatGPT with an existing account:** Tap **Use ChatGPT with an Account**, then follow the onscreen instructions. *Note: To save your chats to your ChatGPT history, you must be signed into an account.*

## If You Don’t See the ChatGPT Option

* Make sure your **Siri Language** and **Device Language** are set to the same, supported language.
* View the list of supported languages and availability here: [apple.co/ai-support](http://apple.co/ai-support).

---

## Sending Requests to ChatGPT Without Confirming

To allow Siri to automatically send questions to ChatGPT:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Apple Intelligence & Siri**.
2. Tap **ChatGPT**.
3. Toggle **Confirm ChatGPT Requests** to **Off**.

*Note: Siri will always ask for your permission before sending any files to ChatGPT.*  
  
You can also bypass confirmation by starting your Siri query with "Ask ChatGPT...".  
  
---

## Turning Off ChatGPT Access

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Apple Intelligence & Siri**.
3. Tap **ChatGPT**.
4. Turn **ChatGPT** off.

If you also want to prevent Siri from suggesting ChatGPT, turn off **Setup Prompts** after disabling ChatGPT.
