# How to delete your account in the ChatGPT iOS app?

Updated: 16 days ago

### Deleting your account is permanent can cannot be undone.

**Deleting your account will prevent you from using the account to access OpenAI services, including ChatGPT and API.** You will NOT be able to create a new account using the same email address. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except we may retain a limited set of data for longer where required or permitted by law.  
  
If you subscribed through the **Apple App Store**, deleting your OpenAI account **does not** cancel that mobile subscription. You must cancel it in the **App Store** or to stop future charges. Please refer to the following article for more information:  
  
  
[How to cancel your Apple subscription for ChatGPT in the ChatGPT iOS app](/en/articles/7905690-how-to-cancel-your-apple-subscription-for-chatgpt-in-the-chatgpt-ios-app)

**Note**: Data retention for certain services may be affected by recent legal developments – please see our [blog post](https://openai.com/index/response-to-nyt-data-demands/) for more details.  
  
To delete your ChatGPT account from the iOS app:

1. Open the ChatGPT app on your device.
2. Navigate to your account settings.
3. Tap **Data Controls**.
4. Select **Delete Account**.
5. Either confirm by clicking **Delete Account** or to change your mind just click **Cancel**.
