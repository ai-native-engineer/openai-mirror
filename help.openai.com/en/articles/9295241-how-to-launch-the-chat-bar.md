# How to launch the Chat Bar

Instantly ask ChatGPT anything using the macOS app

Updated: 10 days ago

You can use the Chat Bar to instantly ask ChatGPT anything, upload files, take a screenshot, or take a photo. By default, to open the Chat Bar using your keyboard, press **⌥+Space:**

![Chat Bar input field with Ask anything placeholder and voice mode button](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-420419f5228f963818190ddb/3a4544aa561a973c37e4b6164a6f939c/Screenshot_2025-04-29_at_15_10_12.png)

You can click and drag the Chat Bar window anywhere on the desktop and you can also open the Chat Bar from the menu bar icon:

![ChatGPT desktop app menu with Open Chat Bar option](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-00169e0b6ab75d7e7ed435e9/51955514f609dbecdfa60d05243474f6/Frame_62.png)

You can start a new conversation with ChatGPT directly in the Chat Bar. To submit your prompt, click the arrow or press the Return button.

![Chat Bar prompt field with a typed question and quick action icons including 4o and voice input](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-978fbcd590e579c0e1ffacad/f171792d35a007bca397d342450c2f69/Screenshot_2025-04-29_at_15_12_02.png)

You can attach a file, photo, take a screenshot, or take a photo when you start a new conversation by clicking on the plus sign:

![Chat Bar plus menu opened with Upload File, Upload Photo, and Take Screenshot options](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-e216512d073755bff7454aeb/f1ad2fcf037efb60a50fc9f58cbeaab5/Screenshot_2025-04-29_at_15_17_22.png)

You can start a conversation by requesting data analysis on a CSV file, or ask a question about an attached image directly from the launcher.

![Chat Bar with data-report.csv attached and the prompt Can you summarize this report?](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-fffd168824273352394d3ea9/8b791178fbbf71fec4dc32f40015f635/Screenshot_2025-04-29_at_15_13_35.png)

You can change the Chat Bar shortcut in the Settings menu:

![Chat Bar settings with the keyboard shortcut set to Option+Space](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-22196710ab499e465e11074d/91a24b20d3535720e5a6b45c8f0f505c/Screenshot_2025-04-29_at_15_15_16.png)
