# Can I Change How I Log Into My Account (Authentication Method)?

Can I Change My Sign-In Method for ChatGPT or the API platform?

Updated: 3 days ago

## Logging in with a different method

* If you originally signed up using an email and password, you can later log in using a social login method (Google, Microsoft, or Apple) as long as the email address matches.
* Your account will automatically combine both login methods, allowing you to log in using either your original email/password or your new social login.

## When you can't change your login method

* If you initially signed up using SSO or a social login method (such as Google, Microsoft, or Apple), you cannot switch to using an email and password later—**unless you are an Enterprise customer, in which case your GTM representative can reset pre-authorization to enable username/password login.**
* Trying to reset your password for these accounts will not work, and no reset emails will be sent.
* Please continue to use the SSO or social login you originally chose to access your account.

* If you signed up with **Sign in with Apple** and chose **Hide My Email**, your account may be tied to Apple’s private relay email address instead of your personal email. To access the original account and any subscription, continue using **Sign in with Apple** with the same Apple ID; using your personal email, a password reset, or another login method may open a different account.

## Managing Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

* MFA is tied to the login method you used when setting it up.
* To switch your login method after enabling MFA:

  1. **Disable MFA first.**
  2. Log out, then log back in using the new sign-in method.
  3. **Re-enable MFA** with your new login method.

⚠️ **IMPORTANT NOTE**: Once MFA is turned on, you must continue using your current login method until MFA is disabled.

## Deleting your account

* Deleting your account does not let you change your login method.
* If you want to use a new login method, you'll need to create a new account after your old one has been deleted

⚠️ **IMPORTANT NOTE:** Deleting your account does **NOT** allow you to change your authentication method.
