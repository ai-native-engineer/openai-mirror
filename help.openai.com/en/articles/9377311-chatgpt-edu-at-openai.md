# ChatGPT Edu at OpenAI

Learn how ChatGPT Edu helps educational institutions deploy AI safely and effectively across campuses.

Updated: 10 days ago

ChatGPT Edu is made for schools that want to deploy AI more broadly to students and their campus communities. [Reach out to our team](https://openai.com/contact-sales/) to learn more.

## What features are available on ChatGPT Edu?

Along with access to our latest flagship model, ChatGPT Edu includes:

* Advanced tools: [data analysis](/en/articles/8437071-data-analysis-with-chatgpt), [web browsing](/en/articles/8077698-how-do-i-use-chatgpt-browse-with-bing-to-search-the-web), and [file uploads](/en/articles/8555545-file-uploads-faq)
* The ability to [build GPTs](/en/articles/8554407-gpts-faq), custom versions of ChatGPT, and share them within university workspaces
* Substantially higher message limits than ChatGPT Free
* Improved language capabilities across quality and speed, with over [50 languages supported](/en/articles/8357869-how-to-change-your-language-setting-in-chatgpt)
* Robust security, data privacy, and administrative controls such as group permissions, SSO, SCIM, and GPT management

## What security and privacy controls does ChatGPT Edu offer?

You own and control your business data in ChatGPT Enterprise. We do not train on your business data or conversations, and our models don’t learn from your usage. ChatGPT Enterprise is also SOC2 compliant and all conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest.  
  
Our new admin console lets you manage team members easily and offers domain verification, SSO, and usage insights, allowing for large-scale deployment into enterprise. See our [privacy page](https://openai.com/api-data-privacy) and our [Trust Portal](https://trust.openai.com/) for more details on how we treat your enterprise data.

## Do you offer free API credits?

In partnership with the NSF-led National AI Research Resource Pilot (NAIRR), OpenAI [provides API credits](https://nairrpilot.org/opportunities/allocations) to researchers focused on AI safety and new applications of AI for improving education, healthcare systems, and climate work.  
  
A portion of this funding is dedicated to supporting applied research and coursework at historically Black colleges, universities, and minority-serving institutions. We also [support researchers](https://openai.com/form/researcher-access-program/) using our products to study areas related to the responsible deployment of AI, mitigating associated risks, and understanding the societal impact of AI systems.  
  
We’ll continue to evolve our offerings in the years ahead to benefit educators, researchers, administrators, and students.
