# How do I delete files I upload?

Understand how uploaded files are stored, retained in conversations, and how to free up space if you hit your file usage cap.

Updated: 10 days ago

*Note*: Data retention for certain services may be affected by recent legal developments – please see our [blog post](https://openai.com/index/response-to-nyt-data-demands/) for more details.

# Manually deleting files

* **Deleting the chat** where the file was uploaded
* **Removing the file** from the GPT builder if it was added as part of a custom GPT's knowledge

# What happens to file content brought into a conversation?

If your file was referenced in a conversation:

* **The relevant content** pulled into context will remain accessible for the life of that conversation.
* This content is only visible to **you**, and **only** within that specific thread.
