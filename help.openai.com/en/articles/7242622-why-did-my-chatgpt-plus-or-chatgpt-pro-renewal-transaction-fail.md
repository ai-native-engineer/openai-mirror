# Why did my ChatGPT Plus or ChatGPT Pro renewal transaction fail?

Updated: 14 days ago

We understand it’s frustrating when a payment doesn’t succeed, particularly when you depend on our service. Renewal payments may occasionally fail due to security measures or issues with the payment method itself. To help you get back on track as smoothly as possible, here are some steps to try:

### Clear Your Browser's Cache and Cookies

Occasionally, your browser's cached data can interfere with transactions. Please try clearing your browser’s cache and cookies and try the transaction again.

### Contact Your Bank

If the issue persists, it may be due to a block or restriction from your bank. We recommend reaching out to your bank to check if there’s an issue with your account, verify if you have sufficient funds in your account, or if your bank is preventing the transaction for security reasons.  
  
If your credit card payment was declined, please refer to our [troubleshooting guide](/en/articles/7232916-why-was-my-credit-card-declined) for more detailed guidance. We recommend verifying your payment details, billing address, and expiration date on your credit card.

### Verify Your Country or Region

Please make sure that both you and your card issuer are located in a supported country. Payments may be declined if either party is outside of the supported regions. For more details, please refer to our [list of supported countries, regions, and territories](/en/articles/7947663-chatgpt-supported-countries).

### Need Further Help?

If these steps don’t resolve your issue, please don’t hesitate to [reach out to support](/en/articles/6614161-how-can-i-contact-support) through our chat widget in the bottom right corner of this page. Our team is here to help and will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.
