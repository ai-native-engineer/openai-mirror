# Where do I access my settings to see my shared links?

How to access the Data Controls setting in ChatGPT to view shared links

Updated: 1 hour ago

Shared Links is GA on Web and iOS.

Android is coming soon!

In [chatgpt.com](https://chat.openai.com/chat) in the top right of the page, click on your profile icon to open the menu then click on Settings:

![Profile menu with Team Org selected and Settings available](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-3ec7e9eda402ec7135386dee/198bf72c446a25c8a5570fe396642d81/Screenshot_2024-05-23_at_12_02_27_E2_80_AFPM.png)

Then in the modal that appears click on **Data controls** > **Manage** > **Shared conversations**:

![ChatGPT Data controls settings with Shared links and a Manage button](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-9b92b4d88d40aafd718c1fba/1d976d2590ddcaded400cd3c0dc3d89b/image.png)
