# Why am I receiving a ‘Suspicious Activity Alert?’

Updated: 3 days ago

OpenAI takes account security seriously. Occasionally, our system will display **‘Suspicious Activity Alerts’** if it detects unusual or potentially risky behavior. Below is an overview of where and how these alerts may appear, as well as tips on securing your account.  
  
These alerts may also impact your access to certain features. Learn more about troubleshooting model feature access issues [here](/en/articles/10258669-troubleshooting-model-feature-access-issues).

## Why You’re Seeing These Alerts

* **Unusual Sign-In Behavior**: Logins from unexpected locations or devices.
* **Inconsistent Usage Patterns**: Sudden spikes in activity or settings changes.
* **Multiple Concurrent Sessions**: More simultaneous logins than usual.

**Note**: These alerts are often cautionary and do not necessarily indicate wrongdoing. They are designed to help protect your account.

## What to Do if You Receive an Alert

1. [**Change your password**](/en/articles/4936828-how-do-i-change-my-account-password)

   * Create a new password that is strong and unique to ChatGPT.
2. [**Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)**](/en/articles/7967234-enabling-multi-factor-authentication-mfa-with-openai)

   * Add an extra step to your login process to enhance security.
3. [**Log out of all devices**](/en/articles/9243857-how-do-i-log-out-of-all-of-my-devices)

### Troubleshooting if restrictions persist

If the banner or feature restrictions continue after taking the steps above, try these self-serve actions:

1. Sign out of all devices, then sign back in.
2. Clear your browser cookies and cache.
3. Try signing in from an incognito/private window, a different browser, or another device.
4. Disable any VPN, proxy, Private Relay, browser extensions, or automation tools.
5. Use one trusted device and network while you troubleshoot.

If the issue persists, contact Support and include:

* The email address on the account
* A screenshot of the banner or any error messages
* Timestamps, with timezone, of when you first saw the alert
* Device, browser, and operating system details
* Whether you were using a VPN, proxy, or Private Relay
* Any request, ray, or device IDs shown in the banner or logs, if available

**Note:** Sharing your account with others may trigger these alerts. For more details, see our [Account Sharing Policy](/en/articles/10471989-openai-account-sharing-policy).

## Tips for Maintaining a Secure Account

* **Use Unique Credentials**: Avoid reusing usernames and passwords across different services.
* **Stay Updated**: Keep your devices and browsers up to date with the latest security patches.
