# Apple Intelligence - Writing Tools FAQ

Updated: 9 days ago

Apple is integrating ChatGPT into experiences within iOS, iPadOS, and macOS with Apple Intelligence, which will allow users to access ChatGPT’s capabilities—including image and document understanding—without needing to jump between tools.  
  
If you choose to allow your Apple device to work with ChatGPT, you can use Writing Tools to access ChatGPT.  
  
You can tap into ChatGPT to access its broad world knowledge within Writing Tools, using it to compose, refine and rewrite. To access ChatGPT within Writing Tools:

1. Open Writing Tools, then tap Compose
2. Describe what you’d like to write in the Compose with ChatGPT field

You can learn more about Apple Intelligence and Writing Tools [here](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/use-writing-tools-iph6f08da1d2/18.0/ios/18.0).
