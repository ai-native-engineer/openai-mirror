# Can I transfer my ChatGPT subscription to a new account?

ChatGPT subscriptions cannot be transferred between accounts.

Updated: 3 days ago

⚠️ We do not support transferring your ChatGPT subscription from one account to another.

If you'd like to use your ChatGPT subscription on a different account, first cancel the active subscription on your current account. Then, purchase a new subscription from the desired account. If you no longer have access to the original account, please contact Support with your billing information so we can cancel the subscription for you.  
  
To transfer conversations to another account, please follow the steps in [Transferring Conversations from 1 ChatGPT account to another ChatGPT account](/en/articles/9106926-transferring-conversations-from-1-chatgpt-account-to-another-chatgpt-account).
