# How to change the phone number associated with your account

Article includes confirmation that changing the phone number for your account is not supported

Updated: 10 days ago

We are slowly rolling out letting new users signup with just a phone number rather than email in the **US and India**. Other countries may have this as limited experimental option.

## How do I change the phone number associated with my account?

OpenAI does not offer the option to change or update the phone number associated with your account. This is true for ChatGPT and the OpenAI API.
