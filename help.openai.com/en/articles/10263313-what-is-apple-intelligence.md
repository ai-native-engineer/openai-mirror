## How do I connect my ChatGPT account to Apple Intelligence?

You can connect free and paid ChatGPT accounts (including Plus, Pro, Team, Enterprise and Edu accounts) with Apple's integrations. On iPhone and iPad, you can set up ChatGPT in Settings.

1. Go to Settings, then tap Apple Intelligence & Siri.
2. Tap ChatGPT, then Set Up.
3. Tap Next, then tap Use ChatGPT with an Account, then follow the onscreen instructions.

On Mac, you can set up ChatGPT in System Settings.

1. Go to System Settings, then click Apple Intelligence & Siri
2. Click ChatGPT, then Set Up
3. Click Next, then click Use ChatGPT with an Account, then follow the onscreen instructions

If you use an Enterprise or Team ChatGPT account and are unable to sign into the Apple Intelligence ChatGPT integration with it, please contact your ChatGPT workspace administrator.

To confirm that your account is connected:

1. Log into ChatGPT
2. Click or tap your profile icon and select **Settings > Security > Secure sign in with ChatGPT**

## What is the Apple Intelligence ChatGPT integration?

Apple Intelligence is the personal intelligence system deeply integrated into iPhone, iPad and Mac that helps users communicate, work, and express themselves. It combines generative models with personal context to deliver intelligence that is most useful and personal, while protecting user’s privacy at every step.  
  
Apple is integrating ChatGPT into several experiences within iOS, iPadOS, and macOS, allowing users to access its expertise — as well as its image and document-understanding capabilities — without needing to jump between tools.

## What can Apple Intelligence do with ChatGPT?

* Siri can tap into ChatGPT’s expertise when helpful. Users are asked before any questions are sent to ChatGPT, along with any documents or photos, and Siri then presents the answer directly.

* Use ChatGPT with visual intelligence to quickly learn more about the places and objects around you.

* ChatGPT will be available in Apple’s system wide Writing Tools, which help users generate content for anything they are writing about. With Compose, users can also access ChatGPT image tools to generate images in a wide variety of styles to complement what they are writing.

## Can I use ChatGPT through Apple Intelligence without a ChatGPT account?

You can always use ChatGPT through Apple’s integrations without logging into a ChatGPT account, to ask questions, get help writing messages, or analyze an image or document. For additional features, like more access to OpenAI’s most powerful AI models, or to save your conversations, you can connect your ChatGPT account to Apple’s integrations.
