# Sign up for ChatGPT Business

Updated: 1 hour ago

***Note:*** *Starting June 24, 2026, Codex seats will no longer be available to new ChatGPT Business plans, or to Business workspaces that never added a Codex seat prior to June 24 2026. Business workspaces that had added a Codex seat prior to June 24, 2026, including pending invites as of June 24 can continue to manage and add usage-based Codex seats.*

## Overview

ChatGPT Business is a self-serve plan designed for organizations that want a shared ChatGPT workspace for their teams. Read more about ChatGPT Business in our overview [article](/en/articles/8792828-what-is-chatgpt-business).  
  
You can start your Business account with either standard [ChatGPT seats](/en/articles/8792828-what-is-chatgpt-business#standard-chatgpt-seats-fixed-cost), or [Codex seats](/en/articles/8792828-what-is-chatgpt-business#codex-seats-usage-based-no-fixed-cost).  
  
After your initial purchase, you will be able to add other seat types.

* Start with standard ChatGPT seats [here](https://chatgpt.com/pricing) (minimum 2 required), or
* Start with Codex seats [here](https://chatgpt.com/codex/team/start) (no minimum number of seats required).

For standard ChatGPT seats, during checkout you can select monthly billing or an annual discounted plan, along with an initial number of seats. For Codex seats, checkout starts with a credit purchase for usage-based billing instead of a monthly or annual seat plan.  
  
When you sign up for ChatGPT Business, you will get access to a ChatGPT Business workspace and the **owner** role. [Read our article](/en/articles/8542216) on managing workspaces, including [workspace roles](/en/articles/8542216-managing-members-seat-types-and-roles-in-chatgpt-business#managing-chatgpt-business-workspaces), inviting members, and other workspace settings.  
  
After completing your purchase and logging into your workspace, you can [invite teammates](/en/articles/8542216-managing-members-seat-types-and-roles-in-chatgpt-business#invite-members), by role and seat type.
