# How can I delete a conversation in the ChatGPT iOS app?

Learn how to remove a past conversation in the ChatGPT iOS app. Deleted chats cannot be recovered.

Updated: 14 days ago

**Note**: Data retention for certain services may be affected by recent legal developments – please see our [blog post](https://openai.com/index/response-to-nyt-data-demands/) for more details.

## Steps to delete a conversation on iOS:

1. Open the **ChatGPT** app on your iOS device.
2. Tap the **menu icon (≡)** in the **top-left corner** to open your conversation history.
3. Find the chat you want to delete.
4. **Press and hold** the conversation title.
5. A pop-up menu will appear. Tap **Delete** (displayed in red).

**Note:** Deleting a conversation is permanent. Once deleted, it cannot be recovered.
