# Apple Intelligence - Visual Intelligence FAQ

Updated: 10 days ago

Apple is integrating ChatGPT into experiences within iOS, iPadOS, and macOS with Apple Intelligence, which will allow users to access ChatGPT’s capabilities—including image and document understanding—without needing to jump between tools.  
  
If you choose to allow your Apple device to work with ChatGPT, you can use Visual Intelligence to access ChatGPT.  
  
Visual Intelligence can tap into ChatGPT to ask questions about the places and objects around you. To access ChatGPT through Visual Intelligence:

1. Open Visual Intelligence with Camera Control
2. Tap Ask
