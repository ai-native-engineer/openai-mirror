# Memory FAQ (Business Version)

Learn how memory works with ChatGPT Business, including merging personal accounts, workspace deactivation, and managing settings.

Updated: 8 days ago

***Note:*** *As of August 29th, 2025, we've renamed ChatGPT Team to ChatGPT Business. For more information and questions related to the name change, please refer to our article:* [*ChatGPT Business Rename FAQ*](/en/articles/12111915)

Organizations and businesses using ChatGPT Business can now use memories to enhance their experience. Memory allows ChatGPT to remember information shared during conversations and provide more personalized, relevant responses over time.  
  
  
[Learn more about how memory works](/en/articles/8590148-memory-faq).

## If I merge my personal account with my Business workspace, will my memories be merged?

Yes. When you accept an invitation to a ChatGPT Business workspace and choose to merge your personal workspace:

* All your data, including conversations and memories, will be migrated to the Business workspace.
* ChatGPT will continue to reference and build on your prior memories under your Business account.
* Merging is irreversible. After merging, your personal workspace will be permanently deleted.

If you choose not to merge, your personal workspace, conversations, and memories will remain separate and private from your Business workspace.  
  
---

## If my workspace is deactivated, will my memories be deleted?

No. Deactivation of a Business workspace does not delete conversations or memories. Instead:

* Data remains intact even if a workspace becomes inactive due to a missed payment or canceled subscription.
* Users will regain access to their conversations and memories if the workspace is reactivated.
* A Business workspace remains accessible until the end of the billing cycle after cancellation or payment failure.
* Workspace owners can reactivate a workspace by paying any outstanding invoices.

[Learn more about workspace deactivation](/en/articles/8798618-what-happens-if-a-workspace-has-been-deactivated).  
  
---

## If my workspace is deleted, will my memories be deleted?

Yes. If a Business workspace is permanently deleted:

* All user data within the workspace, including conversations and memories, will also be permanently deleted.
* Deleted data cannot be recovered.

---

## Can I share memories from my account with other members of my workspace?

No. Memories are:

* Tied to each individual account.
* Not transferable to other users, even within the same Business workspace.

---

## Can I deactivate personalization and memory features for my Business workspace?

Yes. Memory and personalization controls are available at both the user and workspace levels:

* Individual users can disable Memory at any time through their Settings.
* Workspace owners can control whether personalization and memory features are available across the entire workspace. If a workspace owner turns off Memory for the workspace, existing saved memories for members in that workspace are deleted.

---

## Do you train your models with memories?

No. ChatGPT Business follows OpenAI’s Business Terms and Enterprise Privacy policies. By default:

* Workspace data, including conversations and memories, is not used to train OpenAI models.

[Learn more about your data privacy options](/en/articles/7730893-data-controls-faq).
