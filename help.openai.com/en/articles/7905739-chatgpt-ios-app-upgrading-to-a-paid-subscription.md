# ChatGPT iOS app: Upgrading to a paid subscription

Updated: 10 days ago

The ChatGPT iOS mobile app is currently available in the countries listed on the [ChatGPT Supported Countries article](/en/articles/7947663-chatgpt-supported-countries).

If you are an existing ChatGPT user, please log in with the same authentication method (username and password, Google, Microsoft, or Apple). When you're ready to subscribe to a paid ChatGPT subscription, there are a couple options.

# Option 1: Upgrade on the web

Go to [chatgpt.com](http://chatgpt.com), click on your profile menu and select the **Upgrade your plan** option with the available options listed.

![ChatGPT pricing page with Personal plans and the Plus $20/month option highlighted for upgrade](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-50a6282b567dacd745f41b36/fe99189a7408613823fb09bea0038c63/Screenshot_2025-04-29_at_3_12_21_E2_80_AFPM.png)

If you don't see your desired plan or currency available, ensure you have the correct country selected at the bottom of the [pricing page](https://openai.com/chatgpt/pricing/).

# Option 2: Upgrade via the iOS app

***Note:*** *In order to upgrade from Free to Pro you must upgrade from the web.*

There are 2 ways to upgrade from the iOS app:

1. Open and sign into the ChatGPT app and click the **Get** icon at the top of the conversation. This will take you to the Apple subscription payment page to confirm your purchase.

![ChatGPT iOS home screen with the Get Plus button near the top](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-6483b25d2fce6e6a8533b40a/e6d74525531a12fe3ed51b7138c322c0/IMG_4916.png)

1. Click the two horizontal lines in the top left of the app to open the chat history & menu. Then click on your name at the bottom left of the screen to open Settings. From here click **Upgrade.**

![ChatGPT iOS Settings with Subscription set to Free Plan and an Upgrade to ChatGPT Plus option](https://images.ctfassets.net/j22is2dtoxu1/intercom-img-0b1d8bdd7fb00f398e8cdae8/fdc2cea31f024ecf40d775a9204df478/Screenshot_2025-04-29_at_3_29_56_E2_80_AFPM.png)

# For new ChatGPT subscribers

If you've downloaded the iOS app from the App Store but find the subscribe button grayed-out (light purple), this indicates that Apple is still in the process of activating the subscription feature. The message above the button will read, "In-app purchases are currently unavailable. Please try again later". This activation may take up to 48 hours. We appreciate your patience during this time.
