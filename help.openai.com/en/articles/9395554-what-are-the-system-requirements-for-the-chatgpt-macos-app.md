# What are the system requirements for the ChatGPT macOS app?

Understand the system requirements for the macOS app

Updated: 10 days ago

The system requirements for the macOS app are macOS 14 and Apple Silicon (M1 or better).  
  
We will not be releasing a version of the app that is compatible with older operating systems or models.
