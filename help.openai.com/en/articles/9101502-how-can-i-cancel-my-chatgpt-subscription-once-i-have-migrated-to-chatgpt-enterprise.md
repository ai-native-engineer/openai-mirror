# How can I cancel my ChatGPT subscription once I have migrated to ChatGPT Enterprise?

Updated: 3 days ago

When migrating to a ChatGPT Enterprise workspace, individual paid subscriptions associated with the account will be cancelled. You'll also receive a partial refund for the unused period.
