# How can I edit my workspace name or change my workspace image?

Workspace owners can customize their environment by updating the name and image in Workspace Settings.

Updated: 10 days ago

# Who can make these changes?

Only **workspace owners** can update the workspace name or upload a custom image (e.g., your company’s logo).

# How to edit your workspace name or image

1. **Click your profile icon** in the upper-right corner of the screen.
2. Select **Workspace Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. **Hover over your workspace’s name** — an **Edit icon** will appear.
4. Click the **Edit icon** to open a modal.
5. In the modal, you can:

   * Update your **Workspace name**
   * Upload or change your **Workspace image**
6. Click **Save** to apply your changes.

# Tips

* Workspace images must meet supported format and size requirements.
* Changes apply across all team members' views.
