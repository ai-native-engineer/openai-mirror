# Why Was My OpenAI Account Deactivated?

Updated: 3 days ago

We understand that having your account deactivated can be frustrating. At OpenAI, we prioritize the safety, integrity, and responsible use of our services. Account deactivations occur when there are violations of our [Usage Policies](https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies/) or [Terms of Service](https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use/). This article explains the possible reasons for account deactivations and how you can address them.

---

## Why Was My Account Deactivated?

Your account may have been deactivated for one or more of the following reasons:

## 1. Violation of Usage Policies

We prohibit certain uses of our services to promote responsible AI usage. Common violations include:

* Nudity and sexual content
* Child exploitation
* Violence and self-harm
* Deceptive behavior and scams
* Hate, harassment, and spam
* Other inappropriate or illegal content
* Other intellectual property

For more details, please refer to our [Usage Policies](https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies/).  
  
---

## 2. Breach of Terms of Service

Account deactivations can result from activities that violate our [Terms of Service](https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use/), such as:

* Circumventing security or access restrictions.
* Sharing your account or API keys inappropriately.
* Using our services in ways that could cause harm to others or disrupt service integrity.

---

## 3. Security Concerns or Unauthorized Access

If we detect suspicious activity suggesting that your account was compromised, we may temporarily suspend it to protect you and prevent misuse. In this case, we recommend you:

* [Change your password](/en/articles/8485685-i-m-seeing-unrecognized-activity-on-my-openai-account).
* [Rotate your API keys](/en/articles/5112595-best-practices-for-api-key-safety).

---

## 4. Repeated Violations Despite Warnings

We provide warnings for certain policy violations to allow users the opportunity to correct their usage. Failure to address these warnings within the specified time may result in a deactivation.

## 5. Failure to Complete Identity Verification

Some users are required to verify their identity or age in order to continue using OpenAI's services. If you don't complete this verification, your account may be deactivated.  
  
Customers in Italy have 60 days to finish the age-verification process. After 60 days, the account will be disabled until verification is completed.  
  
For more information, see [What happens after I submit my ID for verification?](/en/articles/9981739-what-happens-after-i-submit-my-id-for-age-verification) and [Why am I being asked to verify my identity?](/en/articles/8774323-why-am-i-being-asked-to-verify-my-identity).  
  
If you can't locate your verification email, please contact our support team to have it resent.  
  
---

## Can I Appeal the Deactivation?

If you believe your account was deactivated in error, please submit your appeal using the link in the email notification you received. This allows us to route your request accurately and review it more efficiently.  
  
If you can't access that email, please file an appeal via our intake form: <https://openai.com/form/appeal/>.  
  
If you did not receive an email notification but have lost access to your account, please reach out via the Help Centre chat for support.  
  
Please include the following details:

* User ID and Org ID that you're filing an appeal for
* Any relevant context or details regarding your usage.
* Date range of suspected compromise, if relevant
* Card details if you noticed any unauthorized charges due to unrecognized usage

  + Date(s) of the charge(s)
  + Amount(s) of the charge(s)
  + Last 4 digits of the card charged
  + Brand of the Card (Visa, Mastercard, etc.)
  + Name of the issuing bank
  + Billing Zip Code
* Information about steps you've taken to resolve any issues.

Our team will review your request and respond as promptly as possible.  
  
---

## How to Prevent Future Deactivations

To avoid future deactivations, we recommend:

* Reviewing and adhering to our [Usage Policies](https://openai.com/policies/usage-policies/) and [Terms of Service](https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use/).
* Using our [Moderation endpoint](https://cookbook.openai.com/examples/how_to_use_moderation) to filter potentially violating content.
* Keeping your account credentials and API keys secure.
