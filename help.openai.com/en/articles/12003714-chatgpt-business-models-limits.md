# ChatGPT Business - Models & Limits

Updated: 2 days ago

*As of February 13, 2026, models GPT-4o, GPT-4.1, GPT-4.1 mini, OpenAI o4-mini, and GPT-5 (Instant and Thinking) have been retired from ChatGPT and are no longer available. API access remains unchanged.*  
  
*For more information, please refer to our article:* [*Retiring GPT-4o and other ChatGPT models*](https://help.openai.com/articles/20001051)

***Note:*** *As of August 29th, 2025, we've renamed ChatGPT Team to ChatGPT Business. For more information and questions related to the name change, please refer to our article:* [*ChatGPT Business Rename FAQ*](/en/articles/12111915)

The [ChatGPT Business subscription](https://openai.com/chatgpt/team) is a self-serve subscription plan designed for organizations and businesses wishing to adopt ChatGPT for use among their teams. ChatGPT Business is available for 2 or more users. If you're looking for a larger option, please consider ChatGPT Enterprise.  
  
If you are on ChatGPT flexible pricing, please refer to this [page](/en/articles/11481834-chatgpt-rate-card) for latest rates on our models, features and products.

# Model Picker

In the model picker, **Auto** automatically switches between Instant and Thinking, and you can also choose additional options directly from the top of the model picker.

* **Instant** — instant answers
* **Thinking** — deeper reasoning

Please note that the context window is 128K for Instant and Thinking, and 272K for Pro.

## Note on Unlimited Access

The ChatGPT Business plan offers virtually unlimited messages for the base models. However, usage must adhere to our [Services Agreement](https://openai.com/policies/services-agreement/), which prohibits, among other things:

* Abusive usage, such as automatically or programmatically extracting data.
* Sharing your account credentials or making your account available to anyone else.
* Reselling access or using ChatGPT to power third-party services.

We have guardrails in place to help prevent misuse and are always working to improve our systems. This may occasionally involve a temporary restriction on your usage. We will inform you when this happens, and if you think this might be a mistake, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team. If policy-violating behavior is not found, your access will be restored.
