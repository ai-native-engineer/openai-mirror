# Downloading the ChatGPT macOS app

Updated: 2 days ago

**System requirements:**  macOS 14 and Apple Silicon (M1 or better)  
  
The ChatGPT macOS app is designed to integrate seamlessly into your desktop experience. To get started, download the ChatGPT macOS app:

* <https://openai.com/chatgpt/desktop/>

With a simple keyboard shortcut (**⌥**+Space), you can instantly ask ChatGPT a question. To learn more about how to use ChatGPT on macOS, please read our article about [how to launch the Chat Bar](/en/articles/9295241-how-to-launch-the-chat-bar).
