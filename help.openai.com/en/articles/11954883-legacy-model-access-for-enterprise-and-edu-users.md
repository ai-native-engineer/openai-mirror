# Legacy Model Access for Enterprise and Edu Users

Find the list of currently available legacy models

Updated: 2 days ago

*As of February 13, 2026, models GPT-4o, GPT-4.1, GPT-4.1 mini, OpenAI o4-mini, and GPT-5 (Instant and Thinking) have been retired from ChatGPT and are no longer available. API access remains unchanged.*  
  
*For more information, please refer to our article:* [*Retiring GPT-4o and other ChatGPT models*](https://help.openai.com/articles/20001051)

With GPT-5.5, all Enterprise and Edu workspaces are automatically using our newest models for a faster, more capable ChatGPT experience. If your workflows rely on a previous model, you can turn on legacy model access for a limited transition period. This lets you continue using select older models while you adapt to GPT-5, update custom GPTs, or wrap up ongoing projects before moving fully to the new defaults.  
  
Legacy models available:

* GPT-4o
* OpenAI o3
* OpenAI o3 Pro
* GPT-4.1
* GPT-4.5

These models will appear in the model picker only if legacy model access is turned on for your workspace or account.  
  
Please note that legacy models are still subject to [credits and limits](/en/articles/11487671-flexible-pricing-for-the-enterprise-and-team-plan).

# Enabling access to your workspace

ChatGPT workspace admins can enable access to legacy models in the [workspace admin settings](https://chatgpt.com/admin/settings).  
  
To enable:

1. Click on your profile icon on the bottom-right of the screen.
2. Select **Workspace settings** in the pop-up menu.
3. Under Models, toggle on **Enable legacy models** to enable access to legacy models in your workspace.
