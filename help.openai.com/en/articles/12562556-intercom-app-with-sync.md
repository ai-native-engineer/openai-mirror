# Intercom - app with sync

Updated: 10 days ago

## Overview

The Intercom app with sync lets ChatGPT users securely access and interact with their Intercom data—including conversations, tickets, and help center content—directly within ChatGPT. Once connected, you can reference your Intercom data to get quick answers, draft responses, or analyze trends without switching tools.  
  
  
**Type:** app with sync  
  
  
**Where you can use it:** Chat, Deep Research  
  
  
[**Learn more about connecting a new app**](/en/articles/11487775).

## Example prompts

* “Summarize all open conversations from the past 24 hours in Intercom.”
* “Draft a response to the latest customer ticket about billing issues.”
* “Pull recent help articles related to onboarding.”

[**Learn more about app use cases and prompts.**](/en/articles/12084614)

# Capabilities and Permissions

## What it can access

* **Supported:**

* **Not indexed:** large binary attachments (e.g., videos), embedded media, or unsupported file types.

## What it can do

* Read‑only access to authorized Intercom data via the app.
* Supports both synced indexing and on-demand fetching of current ticket data
* No write actions**:** The app cannot create, edit, or delete Intercom objects.

## Known Limitations

* Initial sync time depends on your Intercom data volume (may take minutes to hours).
* Non‑document content such as images and videos may not be indexed.
* Permission changes in Intercom may take effect after the next sync cycle.
