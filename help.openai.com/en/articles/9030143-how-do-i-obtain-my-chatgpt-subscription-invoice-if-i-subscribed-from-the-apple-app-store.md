# How do I obtain my ChatGPT subscription invoice if I subscribed from the Apple App Store?

Updated: 10 days ago

If you subscribed to ChatGPT through the Apple App Store, the method to access your invoice differs from that used for the [web version](https://chat.openai.com). You can easily view your receipt through your Apple account:  
  
Via iPhone or iPad:

1. Open the Settings app on your iOS device.

1. Tap on your name at the top to access your Apple ID settings.

1. Select "Media & Purchases", then "View Account."

1. After authenticating your Apple ID, if prompted, tap on "Purchase History."

1. Here, you'll find all your purchases and subscriptions. Locate your ChatGPT subscription to view the receipt and other relevant details.

Via iTunes on a Computer:

1. Open iTunes and navigate to the "Account" menu.

1. Click on "View My Account," and sign in if necessary.

1. In the "Purchase History" section, click on "See All"

1. Find your ChatGPT subscription to access more details, including your receipt.

Apple sends an email receipt each time your ChatGPT subscription renews. This email is sent to the address linked with your Apple ID. You might want to check your email inbox for any receipts from Apple concerning your ChatGPT subscription.
