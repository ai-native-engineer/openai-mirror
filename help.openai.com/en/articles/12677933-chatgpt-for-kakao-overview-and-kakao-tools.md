# ChatGPT for Kakao: Overview and Kakao Tools

Updated: 9 days ago

Kakao is offering a ChatGPT Plus subscription promotion. New subscribers through KakaoTalk receive a cashback equal to one month of fees after the third paid month. The promotion is scheduled through **Dec 31, 2025**, and may end early on a first-come basis. Terms and eligibility are managed by Kakao.

ChatGPT for Kakao is a third-party service that lets you use ChatGPT inside KakaoTalk. There is no separate app to install. Open KakaoTalk, go to the Chat tab, then tap the **ChatGPT** button to start a conversation. You can share answers into your chatrooms or ask ChatGPT from within a chat.  
  
ChatGPT for Kakao is co-developed by Kakao and OpenAI. It combines Kakao’s platform and services with the latest generation of OpenAI’s models to provide a natural, chat-like AI experience for daily use.  
  
Existing ChatGPT users can link an OpenAI account for a seamless experience. New users can start in KakaoTalk with a Kakao account, or try ChatGPT without an account where available. If you sign up through KakaoTalk, you can use that same account in the ChatGPT app.  
  
ChatGPT for Kakao is only available in Korea.

## What is ChatGPT

ChatGPT is an AI assistant that helps with writing, brainstorming, learning, planning, and everyday questions. Ask in your own words. You can request quick answers, step-by-step help, summaries, drafts, itineraries, gift ideas, and more. In KakaoTalk, it feels like chatting with a helpful partner.  
  
  
**Examples**

* “Summarize this article in 5 bullet points.”

* “Plan a one-day trip to Busan with kid-friendly stops.”

* “Rewrite this message to sound more polite.”

* “What are three dinner ideas using tofu and mushrooms?”

[Learn more about ChatGPT.](/en/articles/12677804-what-is-chatgpt-faq)

## Account linking and sign-in

* **Link an existing ChatGPT account:** If you already use ChatGPT, link your ChatGPT account to keep track of your ChatGPT for Kakao history in ChatGPT.

* **Sign up with Kakao:** You can start with your Kakao account, and a ChatGPT account will be created using your Kakao account information.

* **Use across apps:** If you signed up through KakaoTalk, that same account also works in the ChatGPT app.

## How to start in KakaoTalk

1. Open **KakaoTalk**.

1. Go to the **Chat** tab.

1. Tap **ChatGPT** at the top of the tab to begin.

1. Type your question and send.

1. To share an answer in a chat, use the share options provided in ChatGPT for Kakao.

1. To ask ChatGPT from an ongoing chat, use the in-chat shortcut where available.

## Kakao Tools and Capabilities

Kakao Tools connect ChatGPT to Kakao services. When your request needs a Kakao service, ChatGPT can call the relevant tool and return results in chat. You stay in control and can choose whether to follow the link or take the suggested action.

## Kakao Map

Find places, see details, and explore nearby options.  
  
  
**What it can do**

* Search for cafés, restaurants, stores, and landmarks

* Show addresses, ratings, and other details.

* Suggest nearby options based on an area you provide

**Try**

* “Where can I get a coffee near Hapjeong Station?”

* “Find a vegan lunch spot within 10 minutes of City Hall.”

## KakaoTalk Reservations

Discover and book with participating businesses that support reservations through Kakao.  
  
  
**What it can do**

* Surface bookable options for services that support KakaoTalk Reservations

* Deep-link you to complete the booking in Kakao

**Try**

* “Want to book an ocean-view hotel in Gangneung..”

* “BBQ near Gangnam Station for tomorrow evening?”

## KakaoTalk Gift

Find gifts, compare ideas, and send through Kakao’s gifting service.  
  
  
**What it can do**

* Suggest gifts by occasion, budget, or recipient

* Link to purchase and send via KakaoTalk Gift

**Try**

* “Gift ideas on Kakao Gift around ₩30,000 for a coworker’s birthday.”

* “Recommend a housewarming present I can send today.”

## Melon

Explore music on Melon, Kakao’s music service.  
  
  
**What it can do**

* Look up songs, artists, albums, and playlists

* Link to listen on Melon (listening requires Melon support in your region and may require sign-in)

**Try**

* “Play the latest album by NewJeans on Melon.”

* “Find a chill study playlist on Melon.”

**Sharing answers to chatrooms**   
  
You can share ChatGPT’s responses into KakaoTalk chatrooms. Use the share controls in ChatGPT for Kakao to post an answer into a conversation, keep context with your friends, or ask a follow-up question while you chat.

## Privacy and data

When you use ChatGPT for Kakao, your conversation is processed to provide the service. If a Kakao Tool is used, relevant details are shared with that Kakao service to fulfill your request. Data handling, retention, and permissions in Kakao follow Kakao’s policies for Kakao and the OpenAI policies and controls apply to the copy of every conversation that is in ChatGPT. [Learn more](/en/articles/7730893-data-controls-faq) about your controls in ChatGPT. Review both sets of terms and privacy notices in your app or on the web.

## FAQ

**Is ChatGPT free in KakaoTalk?**  
  
You can start chatting for free in ChatGPT in Kakao. For additional features or higher usage limits, consider ChatGPT Plus where available.  
  
  
**Can I use voice or images?**  
  
ChatGPT for KakaoTalk currently offers features such as image generation, web search, and Kakao tools, and we plan to gradually expand with more features in the future.Check the ChatGPT interface in KakaoTalk for current options.  
  
  
**Which model does ChatGPT use?**  
  
ChatGPT for Kakao uses the latest generation of OpenAI models provided for this integration. Model availability can change over time.  
  
  
**Will more Kakao Tools be added?**  
  
Yes. Kakao plans to expand Kakao Tools with more internal services and external partners, including public-sector collaborations.  
  
  
**Why can’t I see the ChatGPT button in KakaoTalk?**  
  
You can check it in the latest version of the KakaoTalk Korea app if it’s registered with a Korean phone number.  
  
  
**Why didn’t a Kakao Tool appear when I chatted with ChatGPT?**  
  
Kakao tools may not appear because tools only appear when relevant and supported.  
  
Please re-ask your question with specific details such as the neighborhood, date, time, or budget.  
  
You can also try tapping the Kakao Tools button below the input box before asking, to increase the chance of triggering the tool.  
  
  
**I cannot complete a booking or purchase.**  
  
You may need to sign in to the Kakao service (e.g., KakaoTalk Reservations or Gift) to complete your booking or purchase. If you still can’t complete the action, it might be due to a network issue or item unavailability.  
  
  
**How do I contact Kakao support?**  
  
You can contact Kakao by accessing the [**Kakao Customer Center**](https://cs.kakao.com/helps?service=246)**.**
