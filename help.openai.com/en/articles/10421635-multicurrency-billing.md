# Multi‑currency billing

Learn about which currencies and payment methods you can use to pay for your ChatGPT subscription

Updated: 2 days ago

***Note:*** *This article covers subscriptions purchased from chatgpt.com (web). If you subscribe via the ChatGPT app on iOS or Android, your subscription is managed by the Apple App Store or Google Play and charges will appear in your local currency.* [*API pricing*](https://openai.com/api/pricing/) *can be found here.*

# Overview

OpenAI provides localized pricing to streamline checkout, minimize currency conversion costs, and ensure consistent rates across regions.  
  
This information applies only to standard paid plans. Enterprise and Education plans are managed separately and are outside the scope of this article.

# Payment methods

## All countries

* Credit and debit cards

## UK & EEA

* Bank debit via **Link** (where supported)

## India

* UPI is available for **Go** and **Plus** plans. It is not offered for Pro or Business plans at this time.

## Indonesia

* GoPay is supported for **Go**, **Plus**, and **Pro** users.

## Brazil

* Pix is supported for **Go**, **Plus**, and **Pro** users.

## Korea

* Kakao Pay, Naver Pay, and local cards are supported on ChatGPT subscription checkout pages.

# FAQ

## Can I change the currency of my subscription?

If you already have an active subscription, you’ll continue to be billed in your current [currency](#h_0185dbe62a). If you’d like to switch to a newly supported currency, you’ll need to [cancel your subscription](/en/articles/7232927-how-do-i-cancel-my-subscription-for-chatgpt-plus-or-chatgpt-pro) and re-subscribe once your current billing period ends.

## What if my payment method requires extra verification?

Customers in the EEA may be prompted for **Strong Customer Authentication (SCA)** during purchase or renewal. Follow the on‑screen steps to complete verification.

## Can I purchase API credits in additional currencies?

No. Purchases for API credits are currently limited to USD.
