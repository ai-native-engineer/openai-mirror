# Where can I download the OpenAI ChatGPT iOS app on the Apple App Store?

Link and guidance to find and install the ChatGPT iOS app on the App Store

Updated: 10 days ago

## Where to find the app

You can find and install the official ChatGPT iOS app in two ways:

* **Search**: Open the App Store and search for **"openai chatgpt"** to ensure you're downloading the official app published by OpenAI.
* **Direct Link**: [Download the ChatGPT iOS app here](https://apps.apple.com/app/openai-chatgpt/id6448311069).

⚠️ **Important**: Make sure you are downloading the app **published by OpenAI** to ensure you have the official and secure version.
