# ChatGPT and API services in unsupported countries and territories

Updated: 3 days ago

## How do I use ChatGPT if I am not located in the supported countries?

Accessing or offering access to ChatGPT or the OpenAI API from an unsupported country may result in your account being blocked or suspended.

## How do I pay my bill for ChatGPT if my payment method is not from a supported country?

Using payment methods outside of our list of supported countries will result you being blocked from using our services.  
  
See our [Supported countries](https://platform.openai.com/docs/supported-countries).
