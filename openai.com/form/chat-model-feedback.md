# Chat model feedback

We've deprecated this web form in favor of other channels. You can share feedback about ChatGPT responses or our models’ behavior in the following ways:

* **In ChatGPT**: Click the thumbs down icon below any response to provide feedback, or select Report to flag content that may violate OpenAI’s [Terms of Use⁠](https://openai.com/policies/row-terms-of-use/) or applicable laws. You can also [report⁠(opens in a new window)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/10245791-reporting-content-in-chatgpt-and-openai-platforms) using our [content reporting form.⁠](https://openai.com/form/report-content/)
* **For API customers**: Use the [API dashboard⁠(opens in a new window)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/10306912-sharing-feedback-evaluation-and-fine-tuning-data-and-api-inputs-and-outputs-with-openai) to allow members of your organization to click on the thumbs down icon in Playground.
* **For other issues**: Please contact [customer support⁠(opens in a new window)](https://help.openai.com/en).
