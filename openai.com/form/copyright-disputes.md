## Information

This form is to be used only for reporting alleged infringements of your copyright. By submitting this form, you state that you have a good faith belief that the reported material described below is not authorized by the rights owner, its agent, or the law; that the information contained in this form is accurate; and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner of the copyright at issue. It is illegal to knowingly make false statements in this form.

Please note that we may provide the rights owner’s name, your email and the details of your report to the person who posted the content you are reporting. This person may contact you with the information you provide. You may wish to provide a valid generic business or professional email for this reason.
