# Get involved with OpenAI for Science

Scientific progress depends on collaboration—between ideas, disciplines, and people. [OpenAI for Science](/science/) seeks to connect with scientists and mathematicians who are curious about how AI can accelerate their work, from modeling complex systems to testing new hypotheses. Whether you’re exploring a single problem or building new tools for your field, we’d like to learn from you and collaborate where it can make the biggest impact.
