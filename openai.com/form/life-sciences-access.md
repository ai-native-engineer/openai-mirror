#### We evaluate research beta access based on these principles

#### Beneficial use

Your organization is engaged in legitimate scientific research with clear public benefit, such as advancing biomedical science, improving public health outcomes, or strengthening biodefense.

#### Strong governance and safety oversight

Your organization demonstrates robust governance and risk management, including:

* Clear misconduct reporting and compliance processes
* Insider risk and misuse prevention controls
* A designated individual accountable for safety and oversight

These measures ensure advanced capabilities are used responsibly and with appropriate safeguards.

#### Controlled access and security

Your organization maintains strict access controls and enterprise-grade security systems to prevent unauthorized or inappropriate use, including:

* Granting access only to individuals with a defined, approved need
* Enforcing least-privilege access and timely access revocation
* Protecting systems against account compromise and unauthorized access

This ensures that access to advanced capabilities is tightly managed within a secure, governed environment.
