## OpenAI Economic Research Exchange

The OpenAI Economic Research Exchange supports carefully scoped collaborations with external researchers conducting research on the economic impacts of AI. We invite concise, executable proposals that begin with an important economic question about AI and explain how approved, privacy-preserving use of OpenAI data or context could help answer it.

Applicants whose proposals align with current OpenAI Economic Research priorities may be invited to a follow-up conversation about ways of working and a technical screening for the person who would perform the hands-on analysis.
