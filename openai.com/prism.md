## The standard for scientific writing

Built on LaTeX, Prism brings writing, collaboration, and AI-assisted editing into a single workflow, meeting scientists where and how they already work.

### One workspace for serious scientific writing

Draft, organize, and collaborate in a single cloud-based LaTeX workspace designed for long-form scientific writing. No local installation, no environment management, and no switching between tools.

![Wide view of Prism LaTeX editing interface showing source code on the left and a compiled academic paper preview on the right, with a floating ChatGPT prompt asking what changes to make, displayed against a light blue gradient background.](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/6qHEmmj48sOlDgrgBMKG2E/13190a43319f3732cb513e13abf85be1/P2__1_.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

### Work with unlimited collaborators

Collaborate with as many colleagues as you need using real-time editing and instant previews. Prism eliminates version conflicts, manual merges, and slow compile–debug cycles so teams can stay focused.

![Comment thread interface showing feedback on a document, with one reviewer praising the flow and leaving a small inline suggestion, followed by a reply thanking them, displayed in a dark comment panel against a soft blue gradient background.](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/212dCUCZ4BppeA9BM0DiWl/8981ddf22c25abd1dc3781ba9c039506/P3.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

### AI that understands your paper

Project-aware AI works across the full context of your manuscript, including past drafts and revisions. It helps clarify ideas, check reasoning, and refine structure—updating equations, tables, sections, and references as the work evolves.

![Three rounded action buttons labeled “Summarize,” “Proof read,” and “Find literature,” each with an icon, displayed horizontally on a soft blue gradient background.](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/7e5gGXy5PaO7l2oA0JgNzq/78393e26d2011d447c15553ecae8a473/P4.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

### Save time with built-in proofreading and formatting

Built-in LaTeX rendering, citation management, equation conversion, and automated error checking reduce manual cleanup and formatting work, saving you time and keeping your writing clear and consistent.

![Dark overlay panel displaying a list of academic literature search results, with paper titles, authors, publication year, and journal names, and highlighted author name matches, shown against a soft blue gradient background.](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/1DYAcWHipby4QnHMJMGOVw/1e7ac30fb499b01c34d5c14da47f1817/P5__1_.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)
