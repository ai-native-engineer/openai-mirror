## Flexible deployment options for government agencies

OpenAI offers multiple ways to deploy AI to meet a range of government security and scale requirements.

#### OneGov GSA $1 Deal

Join over 1 million of your U.S. government colleagues using ChatGPT Enterprise for only $1.

* Enable internal workflows across U.S. federal agencies.
* Manage usage with admin tools, access controls, and auditability.

[OneGov GSA Partnership⁠(opens in a new window)](https://www.gsa.gov/about-us/newsroom/news-releases/gsa-announces-new-partnership-with-openai-delivering-deep-discount-to-chatgpt-08062025)

#### FedRAMP controls

ChatGPT FedRAMP and OpenAI API FedRAMP have reached FedRAMP 20x Moderate accreditation.

* Use AI with your agency’s internal data for civilian workloads.
* Scale AI workloads with consistent compliance and governance.

[FedRAMP Moderate announcement](/index/openai-available-at-fedramp-moderate/) [Learn more about FedRAMP⁠(opens in a new window)](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/20001070-chatgpt-enterprise-and-api-platform-for-fedramp?)

#### ChatGPT Gov and AWS GovCloud for sensitive workloads

Use ChatGPT Gov for a self-hosted experience on Azure OpenAI, or use Codex & API deployments in AWS GovCloud (coming soon).

* Extend AI to more sensitive internal workflows, subject to our usage policies.
* Work across agency data in controlled environments.
