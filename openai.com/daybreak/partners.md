Work with OpenAI to bring frontier AI-powered cyber defense into the products and services organizations already trust.

### Build the future of cyber defense

Daybreak brings OpenAI together with cybersecurity companies and service providers to develop practical, governed solutions for defenders.

Together, we can build new workflows, integrate frontier capabilities, and help security teams identify, validate, and remediate threats faster. Additionally, we are joining up with cybersecurity vendors and service providers to help secure agent workflows and Codex across organizations.
