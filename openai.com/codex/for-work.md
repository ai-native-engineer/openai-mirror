#### Go from inputs to deliverables in minutes

Point Codex to your sources and it turns scattered information into clear outputs—briefs, presentations, summaries, and reports—without the back-and-forth.

![Clock history icon.](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/34564l3SBZXgiTM9esNaDV/ca03c014f645e9bb1f8eafd2a0af8060/clock-history.svg?w=3840&q=90)

#### Reduce repetitive work

Offload recurring tasks so updates, reviews, and follow-ups are ready when you need them—without manual effort each time.

![Generate suggested edits icon.](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/5XoHiLJ2cxhyMVbLDyW8wq/8f70630e07eff12dcb316fa474041b8b/generate-suggested-edits.svg?w=3840&q=90)

#### Make space for higher-impact work

Spend less time researching, formatting, building slides, and writing reports, and more time on decisions, collaboration, and strategy. Codex does the work around the work so you can be much more productive.

## A teammate that works your way

Codex adapts to how your team works. Provide context, guide the output, and stay in control of what gets used, shared, or automated.

## How teams use Codex

Try these example prompts to create documents, reports, and updates.

Weekly summary

Create a weekly summary from my calendar, messages, and documents. Highlight priorities, risks, key decisions, and next steps.

Customer insights

Review this survey data in Google Sheets. Surface patterns, pull representative quotes, identify open questions, and draft a concise team update.

Email review

Identify the messages that need a response. Summarize each thread and prepare draft replies, ready for review.

Launch plan

Use this product doc, launch timeline, and Slack discussion to produce a clear launch plan with owners, risks, decisions, and next steps.

Event planning

Compile an attendee list from these stakeholders, draft invitations, set up a tracking sheet, and flag anything that needs a closer look.

Training reminders

Check this training tracker for incomplete items, then draft Slack reminders using the template in this document.

Pipeline snapshot

Compare this quarter's pipeline against last week's forecast. Call out changes, risks, assumptions, and recommended follow-ups.

Project overview

Turn this planning document into a structured tracker, suggest owners based on the discussion, and draft a status update for the team.
