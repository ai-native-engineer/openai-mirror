### A unified workspace for your organization’s systems

#### Work across your business apps

Use apps to complete tasks—like updating an opportunity in Salesforce, analyzing messages from Slack, or researching SharePoint documents—all without switching tools.

#### Bring deeper context to every answer

Apps pull in live details from CRMs, codebases, documents, and analytics platforms so responses are reliable, up-to-date, and grounded in your organization’s data.

#### Control who gets access

Govern which apps are enabled and who can access them, with full workspace visibility.

#### Permissions and control

Review each app’s data access, capabilities, creator, and role-based permissions before enabling them.

#### Audit logs

Track app usage and activity through compliance APIs for monitoring and internal review.

Ensure apps only access data users are authorized to view. By default, no app data is shared or used for model training.
