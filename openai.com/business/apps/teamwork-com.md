## How it works

Let ChatGPT securely access Teamwork.com projects, tasks, milestones, comments, and time entries to answer questions and summarize work.

### Review project and task status

Ask questions about tasks due by project, assignees, tags, and due dates to understand current status using synced Teamwork.com data.

### Summarize activity and comments

Summarize recent comments, task updates, and time entries to understand recent activity across projects.

### Identify open and unassigned work

Identify tasks without assignees, overdue items, or specific tags by querying synced Teamwork.com content.

## Sample prompts

Review project task status

**@Teamwork.com** Show me tasks due in the next 14 days across all projects. Group by project and assignee, and flag anything overdue or blocked.

Summarize recent project activity

**@Teamwork.com** Summarize the last 7 days of activity across projects including key comments, task updates, and time entries. Highlight any risks or decisions.

Identify unassigned and overdue tasks

**@Teamwork.com** Identify all tasks that are unassigned or overdue. Group by project and tag (if available) and recommend a simple triage order.
