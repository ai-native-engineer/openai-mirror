## How it works

Summarize queues, flag urgent cases, and draft responses with customer context, improving resolution speed and consistency across teams.

### Triage tickets and unblock agents faster

List high-priority items awaiting response, summarize new tickets, and generate next-step actions so teams reduce backlog, meet SLAs, and focus on the highest-impact issues.

### Draft accurate customer replies in minutes

Pull the full thread plus key fields, then create a clear follow-up with required steps and tone guidance, helping agents respond faster while reducing rework and escalations.

### Surface trends and recurring issues

Summarize top drivers by different properties, highlight repeat customers, and suggest fixes for common failures, giving leaders faster visibility into quality gaps and workload drivers.

## Sample prompts

List and summarize recent tickets

**@Zoho Desk** List the highest priority tickets that are awaiting our response and summarize what each customer needs.

Identify issues with next steps included

**@Zoho Desk** Identify recurring issues from the last 2 weeks and group them by root cause with a suggested fix owner.

Turn ticket into knowledge base article

**@Zoho Desk** Turn our most common how-to ticket into a knowledge base article draft with clear steps and screenshots placeholders.
