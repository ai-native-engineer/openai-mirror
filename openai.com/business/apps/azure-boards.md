# Azure Boards

Connect to sync Azure DevOps work items and repos for use in ChatGPT.

## How it works

Give teams instant answers from Azure DevOps by querying work items, comments, and projects directly in ChatGPT to reduce status checks, support faster decisions, and keep delivery on track.

### Reduce time spent on project status checks

Ask for assigned work items, due dates, or blocked tasks across projects in seconds, reducing manual searches and recurring status meetings while keeping stakeholders aligned.

### Improve sprint and issue visibility

Summarize bug comments, sprint issues, or tagged work items on demand to spot risks early, unblock teams faster, and keep delivery timelines predictable.

### Enable self-serve insights for teams

Let engineers and managers query Azure DevOps directly for progress, ownership, and trends, reducing dependency on reports and giving teams more time for execution.

## Sample prompts

Summarize progress and key blockers

**@Azure Boards** Give me a sprint snapshot including completed work, carryover, blockers, and the top 5 items I should worry about as a PM.

Summarize threads and takeaways

**@Azure Boards** Summarize the newest high-severity bug threads (include comments) and tell me what the team tried, what failed, and what to do next.

List assigned work

**@Azure Boards** List my assigned work items due in the next 2 weeks, highlight anything blocked, and propose a realistic execution order.
