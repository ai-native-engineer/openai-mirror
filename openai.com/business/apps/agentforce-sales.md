## How it works

Get CRM context on every deal directly in ChatGPT, so reps can research, plan, and close faster; while enriching Salesforce with the key insights so everyone can make smarter decisions.

### Speed up deal preparation

Instantly pull key records and upcoming meetings from Salesforce, then generate call prep, summaries, and next steps that reduce CRM navigation and allow reps to focus on closing.

### Improve CRM data quality

Add researched summaries, notes, and updates directly to Salesforce records to reduce manual entry, keep data current, and ensure teams rely on accurate, shared customer context.

### Strengthen account planning

Create and review account plans using live Salesforce data to align goals, stakeholders, and actions so teams can prioritize effort, coordinate teams, and grow high-value accounts.

## Sample prompts

Prepare customer meeting brief

**@Agentforce Sales** I have a call tomorrow with [customer]. Pull the key CRM context and give me a tight call plan with discovery questions and next steps.

Extract risks and action items

**@Agentforce Sales** Take these raw meeting notes in Salesforce and rewrite them as a clean summary then log the key risks and commitments back to the opportunity.

Identify champions and blockers

**@Agentforce Sales** Identify the likely champion and blockers from our interaction history and suggest a multi-threading plan.
