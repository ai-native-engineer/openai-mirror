## How it works

Edit, refine, and stylize images in ChatGPT by removing backgrounds, adjusting color and lighting, and applying effects, then continue in Photoshop for full creative control.

### Accelerate image editing

Edit and refine images faster using background removal, color adjustments, and creative effects to support quicker asset creation.

### Maintain visual consistency

Adjust color, tone, and style across images before publishing to web, social, or ads to help keep visuals consistent.

### Reduce creative bottlenecks

Enable non-designers to handle routine image edits themselves, reducing design requests and supporting faster turnaround.
