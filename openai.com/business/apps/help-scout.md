## How it works

Let ChatGPT securely query and summarize Help Scout conversations and threads to help teams answer questions and work with real support data.

### Resolve customer issues faster

Summarize recent or open support conversations and draft responses for common issues like refunds, delays, or bugs.

### Improve support team productivity

Search conversations by tag, mailbox, or status to answer internal questions using synced Help Scout data.

### Gain insight from support data

Review patterns across conversations, such as common questions or issues, to better understand support topics without manual review.

## Sample prompts

Draft help content from tickets

**@Help Scout** Turn these conversations into a help center article outline that would prevent repeat tickets, including suggested screenshots to add.

Find relevant policies for replies

**@Help Scout** Find patterns across conversations tagged "refund" in the last 30 days and suggest 3 product or policy changes to reduce volume.

Draft a reply

**@Help Scout** Pull the key facts from this long thread like what happened, what we promised, and what the customer wants, and propose a reply.
