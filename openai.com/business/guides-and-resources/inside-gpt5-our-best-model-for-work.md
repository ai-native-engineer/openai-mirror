## How to evaluate GPT‑5 for your business

![Abstract gradient background blending soft orange, pink, purple, and light blue colors. Centered on the image is a rounded white card containing the text “Analyzing data report,” suggesting an AI or system in the process of reviewing or evaluating data.](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/1Qj6HETb9JG8hIkUIMQzUC/2137f22d62896d937d404a97a1fa904c/how_to_evaluate_GPT5_for_your_business.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

* **Subject-matter expertise** Run tasks across multiple functions-e.g., drafting legal text, producing market analysis, debugging a codebase-then compare GPT‑5's output quality, completeness, and time saved against human benchmarks.
* **Reliable and fast responses** Give GPT‑5 fact-based and multi-step tasks; check factual accuracy, citation quality, and how well it handles clarifications. Measure average response time across low, medium, and high complexity prompts.
* **Advanced reasoning built in** Give the model a complex, ambiguous problem that requires multi-step reasoning with minimal guidance. Assess its solution quality, relevance of follow-up questions, and real-world application.
* **Understands your company context** Upload multiple internal files or connect your company's apps (e.g., Google Drive, SharePoint, GitHub), then evaluate how well GPT‑5 synthesizes answers using your latest content, internal terminology, and guidelines.
