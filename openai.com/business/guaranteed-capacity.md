# OpenAI Guaranteed Capacity

Guarantee long-term access to OpenAI compute for the products, agents, and customer workflows that matter most.

[Plan for capacity](/form/guaranteed-capacity/)[Contact sales](/contact-sales/)

### Scale with capacity you can be certain about

The next generation of AI products will be built by organizations that can move quickly, scale confidently, and bring intelligence into their most important workflows without worrying whether infrastructure can keep up.  
  
OpenAI has made long-term investments in infrastructure, partnerships, and capacity planning to help customers scale compute as demand grows.

OpenAI is now offering **Guaranteed Capacity**, a new way to help customers have certainty of access to compute for their most important products, agents, and customer workflows.

Customers can choose 1-3-year commitments, with discounts that increase based on annual commitment. Guaranteed Capacity includes certainty of access to compute based on spend levels, and customers can draw down from this commitment across the portfolio of OpenAI products.
