# OpenAI Champion Programs

For the people influencing AI adoption across their organization.

Successful AI adoption depends on more than access to tools, it requires people across the organization working together to turn new AI capabilities into lasting business value. Those people are OpenAI Champions.  
  
OpenAI Champion Programs build the workforce capabilities organizations need to lead, deploy, enable, and integrate AI across the business.

### The Champion journey

Whether you’re shaping AI strategy, driving change or building AI workflows for your team, we’re here to connect you with the people and context you need to move adoption forward confidently.

#### OpenAI Enterprise Champion Network

*Open to customers on OpenAI Enterprise plan.*

A dedicated space for deeper engagement with fellow champions and OpenAI.
