### **Kareem**

![Kareem Amin, Founder and CEO, Clay](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/4gn6fJ2u1evdJL5MBvhkuY/f16159286b80a676bf671ba48171b32e/Kareem_1080x1350__4by5__-__3up.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

### **Building Clay**

> “We realized that GTM professionals had lots of creative ideas for how to grow a company that they couldn’t implement. And in order to stay ahead, they constantly need to be doing things differently. So, we decided to build a tool that gives them the ability to express their creativity and iterate quickly.”

Kareem Amin, Co-Founder and CEO

### Turning any growth idea into reality

Kareem approaches go-to-market as a creative discipline. He saw that growth was constrained by systems designed to document the past rather than respond to new ideas.

Clay is built on AI agents that autonomously research markets, analyze signals, and generate outreach strategies in real time. These systems do more than surface insights; they act on them, continuously shortening the distance between idea and execution. The only real limit to what’s possible with Clay is a user’s creativity.

![Kareem Amin, Founder and CEO, Clay](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/2pIdWfBmno1gMnCAPDDqPv/96cbe1566a646bbe71bfeaf0b57fc761/Kareem_1080x1080__1by1__-_2up.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

##### Building with OpenAI

Clay builds on the OpenAI platform to turn raw GTM data into structured signals. OpenAI models are used to classify and extract attributes from company websites, enrich and normalize account data, and interpret sales call transcripts for things like intent and competitor mentions, so Clay can generate actionable insights that power downstream campaign, routing, and sales-prep workflows.

### **Kathleen & Tess**

![Kathleen McMahon & Tess van Stekelenburg, Co-Founders, Valthos](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/13RQuSagt2NINiuo3iTkuS/a5fec878ae6cbc343b656fd722ee402a/Kathleen___Tess_1080x1350__4by5__-__3up.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

### **Building Valthos**

> “As AI advances, life itself has become programmable. Whether these tools are used to usher in a new era of precision medicine or weaponize biology depends on the steps we take today.”

Kathleen McMahon & Tess van Stekelenburg, Co-Founders

### Systems that defend against biological threats

As biology accelerates, the speed of detection and response determines outcomes. Kathleen and Tess founded Valthos to meet that reality.

Valthos operates AI-driven systems that analyze biological sequences as they emerge, assess risk, and prioritize response paths in hours instead of months, strengthening real-world biological defense.

![Kathleen McMahon & Tess van Stekelenburg, Co-Founders, Valthos](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/78XRl6XMZZ1iqiecCw9szU/cd2dd6dadbb9543a4ea8c9ca4a964c98/Kathleen___Tess_1080x1080__1by1__-_2up.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

##### Building with OpenAI

Valthos uses OpenAI as the reasoning layer that ties together biological models, sequence analysis, and decision pipelines. Outputs from specialized bio models are evaluated, compared, and prioritized through test and verification frameworks, enabling rapid in-silico risk assessment and medical countermeasure triage.

### **Building Unify**

> “We’re rebuilding go-to-market from first principles and giving you a system of action to generate pipeline that is repeatable, observable, and scalable.”

Connor Heggie, Founder and CTO

### The system of action for pipeline

Connor believes go-to-market is fundamentally a search problem. The data exists, but teams lack a way to surface the best leads and action on them immediately. Unify is a system of action for turning signals and outreach into a consistent pipeline, so that the best products win.

![Connor Heggie, Founder and CTO, Unify](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/5xzaPFWBiVXo7mlD59Df9x/7bdab5c8e1af978d5802c2c97eed6815/Connor_1080x1080__1by1__-_2up.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

##### Building with OpenAI

Unify relies on OpenAI’s agentic capabilities and tool-calling to execute millions of automated research jobs across your entire market. Always-on agents detect buying signals, qualify accounts in real time, and personalize outreach with live context. This gives reps clear intent and a real reason to engage so every outreach is relevant and grounded in how their product helps.

### **Building Vanta**

> “Security and compliance should be two sides of the same coin. You shouldn’t just check the box for an audit—you should secure your systems first, and let software prove it.”

Christina Cacioppo, Founder and CEO

### Helping businesses earn and prove trust

Christina founded Vanta after seeing how compliance became disconnected from real security.

Vanta runs an agentic trust platform that continuously monitors controls, identifies gaps, and fixes issues in real time, turning trust into an always-on system rather than a periodic snapshot.

![Christina Cacioppo, Founder and CEO, Vanta](https://images.ctfassets.net/kftzwdyauwt9/5CrHr74GfviEsXDraSacZT/d8901ef915e1a133eec834793565f480/Christina_1080x1080__1by1__-_2up.png?w=3840&q=90&fm=webp)

##### Building with OpenAI

Vanta uses OpenAI models to reason and translate data across its security and compliance platform. The models map risks, controls, assets, and evidence into a continuously updated system of record, and assist with interpreting regulations, drafting policy changes, and checking them against new requirements, while Vanta’s own workflows manage validation, approvals, and audit-grade reliability.
