Our 6-month program accelerates talented builders and early researchers from any domain, giving them the mentorship and environment to do real research at the frontier of AI.

## About the program

The OpenAI Residency supports researchers and engineers who are already exploring AI or who come from closely related fields such as mathematics, physics, or neuroscience. Residents work alongside OpenAI teams, gaining hands-on research experience, close mentorship, and the judgment needed to contribute to advanced AI systems.

> “This program is an excellent way for people who are curious, passionate, and skilled to sharpen their focus on AI and machine learning—and to help us invent the future.”

Sam Altman, CEO of OpenAI

## Who we’re looking for

We welcome strong candidates from quantitative and scientific fields, and we are placing a growing emphasis on early builders who are already doing meaningful work. These individuals move quickly from idea to prototype, show strong research instincts, and demonstrate unusual self-direction.

Residents bring their existing strengths to real research problems in collaboration with OpenAI teams, while receiving a full salary. We value excellence regardless of institution or background, and encourage applicants who are self-taught, non-traditional, or early in their careers if they have a clear record of building and learning with intensity.
