# About

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company. Our mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity.

### Our vision for the future of AGI

Our mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence—AI systems that are generally smarter than humans—benefits all of humanity.

### Our structure

OpenAI consists of the nonprofit OpenAI Foundation and the for-profit OpenAI Group. The Foundation governs the Group, which operates as a public benefit corporation. The OpenAI mission advances through the combined impact of both organizations.
